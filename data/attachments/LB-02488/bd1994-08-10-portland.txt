Bob Dylan
August 10 1994 
State Theater  
Portland, Maine 

In the latter part of 2003, I received many recordings of Dylan 
concerts from 1994. While most of these were said to be lowgen
copies, in many instances I do not have any information as to the
taper or equipment used. I have just begun to listen to some of
these recordings (what can I say?). The show seeded here, 08-10-1994 
Portland, is the first one I listened to and it struck me as a 
nice recording of a date which is not represented by a commercial
boot (as far as I know). At least I enjoyed it; I hope that some 
of you do as well.
