Bob Dylan and his band 
Washington D.C.
9:30 Club
April 2, 2004

Got this as aud disks in june 2004- extracted by EAC - no errors.
No info on taper identity or alias then -
only info on lineage was that it's recorded with Schoeps Mk4 mics.
Research later on indicated it probably was a NET Taper E recording.

It is a clear upgrade compared with LB-1682 (Theodore recording) - and
based on disc running times probably a different recording from LB-1793 -
although I have not got that one for comparison.

Excellent sound.
