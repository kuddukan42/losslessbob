Improved Air Remasters

Some history cribbed directly from the creator of this fine series: The fan who began Improved Air Remasters did so for the simple reason of wanting to hear more natural-sounding recordings.  His process, and he works alone, of remastering includes restoration and light or hard tweaking of the original source, among other things. Obviously each new project is a different case. For ease of trading he regrets not foot printing his sets early on, but may list out the cd times one day. He is currently working on an ambitious and exciting project, namely remastering the entire USA/Canada Spring 1997 Tour.
