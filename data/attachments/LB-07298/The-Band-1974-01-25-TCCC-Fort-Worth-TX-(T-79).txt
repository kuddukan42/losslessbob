The Band 1974-01-25 TCCC Fort Worth TX (T-79) from the original analog tapes


I'm the guy who recorded this show.  I have the original analog cassette tapes of it.  Over the years I have traded analog tape copies but until now have never done a digital transfer of it.  This is the first and only time it has been digitally transferred from the original analog cassette tapes.

I have also done the artwork for this version of this show from an original drawing by Jonathan Bock.  Please keep the artwork and text file, unaltered, together with the SHN's.

Dylan & The Band would alternate sets sometimes Dylan would play with The Band and sometimes alone.  Other times The Band played their songs without Dylan.  I have untangled the sets and present here just the songs on which The Band played without Dylan.



 1.   Stagefright
 2.   The Night They Drove Old Dixie Down
 3.   King Harvest (Has Surely Come)
 4.   When You Awake
 5.   I Shall Be Released
 6.   Up On Cripple Creek
 7.   Rag Moma Rag
 8.   This Wheel's On Fire
 9.   The Shape I'm In
10.  The Weight
