Tarrant County Convention Center, Fort Worth, Texas, 25th Jan 1974

Master / Low gen tape > CD > EAC > Flac
There are two excellent but incomplete tapes of this show which when combined cover the full show. One tape is only the Dylan set (in stereo), the other covers tracks 1-22. 
IMHO The stereo tape is one of the best quality audience tapes of the tour.
