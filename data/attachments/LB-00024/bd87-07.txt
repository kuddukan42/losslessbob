=====================================
Bob Dylan with the Grateful Dead 7/87
=====================================

This is a 7CDR set of the 6 show sets that Bob Dylan performed with the Grateful Dead in July 1987.  This is a much criticized collaboration but some will find it worthwhile. I recommend this for those who liked the rehearsals and the official release. The set lists are much more repetitious than the rehearsals and the sound quality is not as good as the official release and varies between shows. However sound quality on all of this set is much better than what circulates on bootleg cd fron these shows.

The majority is from unknown soundboard sources with audience source used to fill in the missing songs. This is complete for the Dylan on stage except there is some discrepancy in the literature whether Dylan was on stage and playing guitar for the Touch of Grey on 7/10/87 which is missing from this set.

The music was edited by patching over and snipping out the occasional drop and static to make them less obvious.
