Carnegie Hall 26 Oct 1963

I decided to assemble all 12 known tracks from best possible sources,
then place the tracks into the correct order and add any intros.

1. The Times They are A-changin  (1)
2. Ballad of Hollis Brown (1)
3. Who Killed Davy Moore (2) with intro from (4) added
4. Boots of Spanish Leather (1)
5. John Birch Society Blues (3)
6. Lay Down Your Weary Tune (4)
*7. Blowin'in the Wind - not in Circulation
8. Percy's Song (5) with intro from (4) added. Aud. coughs not audible on other sources.
9. Seven Curses (5) (has the complete end. (4) & GBS 2 are abrupt)
*10. Walls of Red Wing - not in Circulation
11. North Country Blues (1)
12. A Hard Rain's A-Gonna Fall (2) (intro to 19 deleted)
*13. Talking WWIII Blues - not in Circulation
*14. Don't Think Twice, It's Alright - not in Circulation
15. With God on Our Side (1)
*16. Only a Pawn in Their Game - not in Circulation
*17. Masters of War - not in Circulation
*18. The Lonesome Death of Hattie Carroll - not in Circulation
19. When the Ship Comes In (2) (intro from 12, intro to next track deleted)

Sources used: 
1. Columbia CSK 17254 promo Live at Carnegie Hall 1963
2. Columbia C2k93937 Bootleg Series 7
3. Columbia C3747382 Bootleg Series 1-3
4. In Concert- Colosseum boot
5. Fantasy Acetate 77182 - Improved AiR Remaster.

I checked other sources: In concert - Capricorn boot, 3 Bob Dylan.com tracks, 
Live in NY 1963 - Black Panther, Genuine Bootleg Series 2 & 3 but these all
turned out the be the same or inferior quality to those used.

The order for the concert in given in "On the Tracks vol 4, No. 1".
