Basement Tapes
Hollow Horn Bootleg "Safety Master" plus 4 bonus tracks

This new boot was first posted elsewhere today and because it runs almost 3% slow, I wanted to pass it along at a better speed. (I didn't correct the last 4 'bonus' tracks.) Also fixed one glitch and a few clicks, and cleared up the small dc offset. Some uncorrected faults are noted below.

It contains a fair amount of uncorrected clipping (digital overload), and also contains add-on leakage from an unrelated source on track-16 and beyond. The last track is, I believe, from the earlier "sub-basement" sessions.

Please do not reseed elsewhere until next month.

/Lowgen
