Bob Dylan, Memphis Mid-South Coliseum, 23Jan1974
Mono audience recording 73:09. Complete Dylan set.

This is a hot show -- the recording is below average for the time.

This transfer from the Lowgen Archives was never seeded before Jan 2006. Please do not reseed elsewhere without this info file and all relevant comments.

The first 3 tunes are from one taper - tracks 4+ from another.  They were collected in '74 but neither tape is 1st gen. Both tapes have been eq'd to eliminate mid-range harshness from the 1st and way too much bass in the 2nd. Use your tone controls for custom adjustments, of course.
