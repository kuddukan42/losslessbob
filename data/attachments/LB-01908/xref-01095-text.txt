 3. If You See Her, Say Hello
 4. Tweedle Dee & Tweedle Dum
 5. If Not For You
 6. Drifter's Escspe
 7. The Man In Me

 1. Honest With Me
 2. Forever Young
 3. Summer Days
 4. Don't Think Twice, It's All Right
 5. Like A Rolling Stone
 6. All Along the Watchtower
 7. Highway 61 Revisited


N.B.

Track 12 on disc 1 and tracks 2 and 4 on disc 2 were digitally processed to eliminate crowd noise (the noise was mostly in the right channel; the right channel was deleted and the left channel was copied as the right channel, and some reverb added).  The original tracks (without any processing) are included, marked with the postscript "A" (e.g. bd040627d1t12A).  If preferred, they can be substituted for the processed tracks.

Also there was an intermittent problem with the sound system at the venue.  It can be heard at the near the end of Honest With Me, Like A Rolling Stone and Highway 61 Revisited.
