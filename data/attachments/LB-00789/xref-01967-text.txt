I have been thinking of doing a tribute to Artur J 
Artur Jarosinski was from Poland, a bright star who tragically died on the way home from a concert in 2011 aged only 32. 
He sent me (among other things) four CDRs, that he had assembled, and I would like to give them wider circulation.
This is #4 of 4 


19 July 1994
Warsaw, 
Poland, 
Kongresshalle
LB-0798

1. Jokerman, 
2. Just Like A Woman, 
3. All Along The Watchtower, 
4. You're A Big Girl Now, 
5. Tangled Up In Blue, 
6. Under The Red Sky, 
7. Mama, You Been On My Mind (acoustic w band), 
8. Masters Of War (acoustic w band), 
9. Love Minus Zero/No Limit (acoustic w band), 
10. God Knows, 
11. In The Garden, 
12. Maggie's Farm, 
13. Ballad Of A Thin Man, 
14. It Ain't Me, Babe (acoustic w band), 
15 Blowin' In The Wind (acoustic w band), 

When I played D2 of Artur's CD's I discovered D2 was from some other show.
I Substituted the last 4 tracks from the Schubert remaster of the same show LB-2898.
Consequently the Bonus tracks are absent.
