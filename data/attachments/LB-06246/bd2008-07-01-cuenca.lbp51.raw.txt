Bob Dylan

Complejo Deportivo La Fuensanta
"La Fuensanta"
Cuenca, Spain
July 1, 2008

I received a pair of external mics and battery supplies from Jantemp, but they just arrived while I was in Gredos, so I could only use them to tape in Cuenca. It was a great show and the recording turned out quite well, indeed, (except for the clipping on loud parts, which is my fault) but I sadly found what was one of the worse crowds ever! Those talking idiots never shut up, showing such disrespect for Dylan's work! But it's all right, ma.... I was only taping.

Some will find bothering those clipping parts and maybe more problems here and there, but sound is mostly clear and powerful, so we can still be aware of the amazing show Bob delivered in Cuenca last night. This is the raw version. I didn't harm the sound of the recording in any way.

Enjoy!
