International Arena 
National Exhibition Center
Birmingham, England
7 June 1989
 

Pretty nice quality audience recording, up front and in your lap which is just what is needed for a rocky show 
such as this one. At the height of his 'power-pop' era, this show has always been one of my favourites from 
40 yrs of watching Bob. I shall be putting up two versions, this one and the recording used for the Wanted Man CD. 
This one is loud and in your face like I say, the other is a bit more distant but still very nice.

This recording is a little bit over-cooked during Subterranean but still quite listenable to, volume settles down 
until just the very beginning of Silvio.
