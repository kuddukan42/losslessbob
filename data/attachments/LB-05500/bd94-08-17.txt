Bob Dylan
August 17 1994 
Park Arena  
Hershey, Pennsylvania 

Taper: NET Taper K.

Source: Audio disks of unknown origin > (dBpowerAMp set to
compression level 6) flac.

Note: This is another show which does not appear to have an entry at
the LB archives. My good friend cevau provided the disks in
response to a request from a taper/collector friend who was missing
this show. I suspect that others might be missing the show as well, 
hence my decision to put it up here at HungerCity. If anyone has a
an alternative, complete  recording of this date, please share if 
possible. I received the show with no information as to the taper,
but the fragmentary nature of d1 t1 made it easy to recognize that
it must be the NET Taper K recording (thanks to the incredible knowledge
of HG!).
