============


audiowhore repair project - 1976

source used: LB-61 (Bob Meyer's master > CDR > EAC > FLAC)
audience recording
there are no alternate versions or sources listed on LosslessBob


Notes about this recording:
- all thanks to the taper for being there
- this is the first recording of the 1976 tour to capture the complete concert (other than some small parts missed during tape flips)
- pitch was too high and speed too fast
- after being speed corrected, the total time of this show is now 8 minutes longer than the original circulating version
- nothing from this show has been officially released.


What i did:
- pitch and speed correct each track individually - they varied significantly throughout
- remove SBEs, other gaps, two repeated crowd sections and numerous microphone bumps
- eq mixing
- volume adjustments to balance left and right channels
- No noise or hiss reduction used by me (but it sounds like it was used during previous processing)


============

If burning to CD, suggested track splits are:
1 = tracks 1 to 13 (56:50 mins)
2 = tracks 14 to 29 (72:01 mins)
3 = tracks 30 to 43 (78:11 mins)

total time: 3 hours, 27 mins, 02 seconds.
