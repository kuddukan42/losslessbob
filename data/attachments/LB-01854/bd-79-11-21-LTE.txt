Bob Dylan
79-11-21
Santa Monica
Offmaster audience recording.
Taper: LTE

The "Gospel Project" uncovered only a single tape of this show, which consists of just
the Dylan set. The girls'songs are missing. The offmaster version I am seeding is doubtless
this same recording. The commercial boot "Preaching to the Converted" derives from this
source. I do not have that boot with me here in Japan, and cannot comment on its sound 
quality in comparison to the present offmaster version.
