Bob Dylan
Philips Arena
Atlanta
Georgia, USA.

9th February 2002


This came to me in a trade some time ago so cannot be sure of the lineage
but I believe that it was released by Mainstream in early 2003.
I ripped the discs using EAC and used TLH to convert to flac.

As far as the quality of the recording is concerned, it is stunning.
It seems to me to be a soundboard as the audience is very quiet, in fact
it is bareley perceptable between the tracks (even for a soundboard!!).

I'm sure that there must be a number of people out there who don't have this 
one, so if you don't, grab it and enjoy it.
