A hodgepodge of a handful of my favorite and best sounding early to mid '90s shows featuring "Positively 4th Street". The five shows in question, all currently in the process of being upgraded, are as follows:

May 2, 1992  Sonoma County Fairgrounds (Santa Rosa, California)

November 9, 1992 Van Wezel Performing Arts Center (Sarasota, Florida)

March 16, 1995  Stadthalle  (Bielefeld, Germany)

April 17, 1996 University Of Vermont (Burlington, Vermont)

November 20, 1996  Michigan State University  (East Lansing, Michigan)

For further information, including Setlist and Artwork, please refer to the Additional Notes provided below.

Olof - Dylan had sung the first two lines of Mr. Tambourine Man when a woman from the audience came on stage and ran towards Dylan. He looked startled and jumped back. The band continued to play and the woman "sang", repeating a few lines several times. She tried to get Dylan to sing with her but he stayed back. Given the length of this performance it was obvious that security allowed her to stay on stage. When they came to lead her off, Dylan took her arm and raised it above her head as in triumph! The same woman reappeared and ran to the microphone when Dylan was starting "Blowin' In The Wind". She said a word or two but security took her off stage immediately this time.
