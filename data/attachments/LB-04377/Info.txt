Mono Mixing Desk Recording (Incomplete)

This recording circulates, I believe, in two other forms with differing
track lists, one being the commercial bootleg "Rio".

NOTE: I was told in good faith when I was sent the tape in 1990 that it
is 3rd generation and have no reason to doubt that information. It came from
a generous and very reliable source as with most of my tapes.

When transferring I found the right channel to be considerably better than
the left so have used dual right channel. As with all my transfers no
Normalisation or other alterations have been made to the sound unless stated.
