Bob Dylan, 5/16/03, Birmingham Alabama, 15th Annual City Stages, Miller Lite Stage, 2CDR

(48min+77min), The emergence of this long awaited and much sought after recording is due to the tireless efforts of Ben from Alabama, to whom we all owe a very large debt of gratitude! Thank you my friend!, Ben has worked relentlessly for months to locate a decent recording of this show only to find a handful of incomplete recordings He has, however, stitched together 2 of these incomplete sources finally providing us with a complete documentation of the event. This will be a 3 disc set and will include Bob's complete performance as well as The Waifs show; excellent sound
