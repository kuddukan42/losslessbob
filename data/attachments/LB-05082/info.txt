Bob Dylan
August 22, 1989
Sandstone Ampitheatre, Bonner Springs, KS
Audience recording
Sony D-6 w/ Sonic Studios>1st gen cassette>Creative>CDWave to split tracks>Flac

I would rate the sound as VG. I have included sound samples. There is crowd noise at times, particularly during the quieter acoustic numbers. The third sample is a pretty good example. Every once in a while there is some enthusiastic yelling, but no regular screamers or anything really ongoing and annoying. I have a lot of Bob shows, but I come back to this one from time to time. Hollis Brown is a standout. 

I traded this a few times, but never on CDR, so I think this is as close to the master as it is going to get for this source. I have not heard a better source for this show, but if you have one, please post it. 

Sorry I didn't catch the exact band lineup.
GE Smith is on guitar
Tony Garnier is on bass
Correct me if I'm wrong
