Bob Dylan
1996-07-12
Stadthalle  Magdeburg, Germany

The commercial boot "Eyes of the Idol" derives mainly from this concert. "Eyes" is highly 
regarded by "BobsBoots", so perhaps the present offmaster audience recording is not an
upgrade to that boot (I do not have "Eyes of the Idol" with me at present in order to make
any comparative judgement). Nevertheless, this is an excellent recording of an outstanding
performance.
