
Bob Dylan, Champlain Valley Exhibition grandstand, Essex-Junction, VT
(with Willie Nelson and John Mellencamp)
July 17 2009

Taper:  Micro-Magnon, the stone-age taper.

Lineage: Creative JB3 recorder at 44 khz, Sound-Pro Mics, battery box set at 107hz bass roll-off. Normalized in SoundForge, split with CDWave, FLAC level 8.

Set list:




Leopard-Skin Pill-Box Hat (Bob on guitar)
Seńor (Tales Of Yankee Power) (Bob on guitar) 
I'll Be Your Baby Tonight (Bob on guitar) 
Rollin' And Tumblin' (Bob on keyboard) 
Spirit On The Water (Bob on keyboardand harp) 
Things Have Changed (Bob on keyboard) 
If You Ever Go To Houston (Bob on keyboard) 
High Water (For Charley Patton) (Bob on keyboard) 
I Feel A Change Comin' On (Bob on keyboard) 
Highway 61 Revisited (Bob on keyboard) 
Nettie Moore (Bob on keyboard, Donnie on banjo) 
Summer Days (Bob on keyboard) 
 
Encore cheers
Like A Rolling Stone (Bob on keyboard) 
All Along The Watchtower (Bob on keyboard) 


I don’t tape very often, and this wasn’t an easy night.  It was raining, and miserable and all.  But I think I did OK with this one.

I also taped Willie Nelson and John Mellencamp, both of whom provided great music to round-up a very pleasant evening.  I didn’t regret a thing.  Too bad beer drinking was limited to a tent.  I didn’t realize Vermont was so strict..  Man I love living in Quebec, where drinking beer is a God-given right at a show!

I will upload Willie and Mellencamp when enough seeders take over.
