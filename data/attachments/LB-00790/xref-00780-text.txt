Bob Dylan (with Tom Petty & The Heartbreakers)
Johanneshovs Isstadion
Stockholm, Sweden
1987-09-26

My first upload here!

The source for this is an audience tape transferred to CDR.
I bought the CDR a few years ago from some pretty "high-level" Dylan traders, so I'm inclined to think it was made from a very low-gen tape, if not from the Master even

Sound quality is pretty OK, comparable to Stockholm '81 which was recently uploaded herecould perhaps be improved with a little "tweaking".
Not widely circulated I believe, but perhaps mostly of interest to completists, or for those who were there (like me!)

lineage: AUD tape > CDR > FLAC (XLD-secure mode) > ffp files with xACT

technical note: there was a scratch on track 10 of the CDR, which shows up as some "damaged sectors" in the log file, but there's nothing audibly wrong to my ears.
