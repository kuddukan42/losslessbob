1984 Project
Mick Taylor with Bob Dylan
1984 Tour Rehearsals

Well, my last seed got banned, sorry about that (it was a grand one).  Anyway, here is the 
beginning of the 1984 project.  It’s the tour rehearsals for the 1984 Bob Dylan tour.  
There are some interesting songs here on this one from the Fendert Artisan Archive.  
Enjoy!
