Dave's notes:
well i was at this one and it was great! the 1999 run continues with this show, notable for the live debut of Pass Me Not O Gentle Saviour, which when it was played no-one had any idea what song it was...at least where i was there was much whispering "which tune is this??" pretty cool. sound is not quite as good as the last 1999 show i upped but it is still very respectable and listenable. everything is clearly represented in the mix, perhaps the drums are a tad low but who can complain? judicious use of EQ to add some highs will bring this up to a solid EX- aud recording. Paul James plays on the encores, except "Blowin'" i find it extraordinary that he turned down Bob's invitation to be his guitarist, but as the Bobtalk says "he had his own thing he had to do." more power to him. i see Paul every six months or so at the club i work at, doing his bluesy takes on Dylan and others. anyway Sphere had requested some 1999 shows so i will upload all i have in the next few weeks. i find 1999 under-represented on my list, unlike 1996 or 2003. just the way things go i guess. anyway i hope you all enjoy this show like i did when i was there.
