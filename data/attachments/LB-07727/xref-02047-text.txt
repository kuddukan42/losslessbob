=== Bob Dylan 2009-04-25 London.txt

Bob Dylan

The O2 Arena
London, England
25 April 2009

-----------

This is a remix/fix of LB-7727 which was sourced from the bootleg 'Bob Dylan and his band - LIVE AT THE O2 ARENA'.
That boot was issued by Silent Sea Records in August 2009 and was stated to be a 'soundboard recording'.
The bootleg artwork credited this as 'Recorded by Ossie'. So, thanks to Ossie.
The original bootleg recording was hissy and had some flaws. 
For example, there was some brief moments of static and what sounded like a radio not tuned in correctly.
I remixed it to try and improve the sound and lessen those flaws where possible.
While it is clearly not an audience tape, i'm doubtful that it was recorded from the soundboard as claimed.
Somewhere / somehow in between I suspect.
The sound is very good but not perfect.
It is an incomplete recording that did not include 6 songs from this show (see below for those missing tracks).

-----------

1. Maggie's Farm
2. The Times They Are A-Changin'
3. Things Have Changed
4. Chimes Of Freedom
5. Rollin' & Tumblin'
6. The Lonesome Death Of Hattie Carroll
7. 'Til I Fell In Love With You
8. Highway 61 Revisited
9. When The Deal Goes Down
10. Thunder On The Mountain
11. Spirit On The Water (beginning cut)
12. band intros
13. Blowin' In The Wind

===========

Note: tracks 7, 8 have some flaws / static / tuning noises that i could not fix.

Missing songs from this recording:
 - Workingman's Blues #2
 - Ballad Of Hollis Brown
 - Po' Boy
 - Honest With Me
 - Like A Rolling Stone
 - All Along The Watchtower

(complete audience recordings of this show were shared by spot, EBR and other tapers soon afterwards.)
