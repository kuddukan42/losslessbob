Bob Dylan, 5/2/92, Sonoma County Fairgrounds (Santa Rosa, California), 2CDR

(57min+43min), from "Positively Potpourri" set; Artwork - http://home.wanadoo.nl/piotr.tymstra/graphics/images/santarosa_020592.jpg, Olof - Dylan had sung the first two lines of Mr. Tambourine Man when a woman from the audience came on stage and ran towards Dylan. He looked startled and jumped back. The band continued to play and the woman "sang", repeating a few lines several times. She tried to get Dylan to sing with her but he stayed back. Given the length of this performance it was obvious that security allowed her to stay on stage. When they came to lead her off, Dylan took her arm and raised it above her head as in triumph! The same woman reappeared and ran to the microphone when Dylan was starting "Blowin' In The Wind". She said a word or two but security took her off stage immediately this time.; excellent sound
