BobTalk
Heres an autobiographical song for ya. (before It Takes A Lot To Laugh. It Takes A Train To Cry)
Heres a true story, could happen to anybody. Its called Isis. I wanna dedicate this to Keith Richards.
I wanna say hello to Rusty. Rusty are you there? OK. (before Simple Twist Of Fate)
How about a hand for Luther Rix, Howie Wyeth, Rob Stoner and Scarlet Rivera  Mr. Soles playing on that  hell be greatly honored I hope  gonna do a song about a man who is in jail right now in New Jersey. (before Hurricane)
Thank you. Were gonna close with this song, an old Woody Guthrie song called This Land Is Your Land.

Notes
 This tape started circulating in 1999. It contains the Dylan set plus the other artists songs.
 First and only Fourth Time Around during Rolling Thunder Revue.
