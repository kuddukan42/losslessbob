Reno, June 11, 1986 - taper markp - 2 discs
Bob Dylan
Lawlor Events Center, Reno, NV, June 11, 1986

Source/lineage information: taper markp, transfer from the master cassettes
Nakamichi CM300(cp4) > Sony tcd5m > nakamichi cr-1a > hhb cdr830 >
 EAC > single WAV > track breaks edited via cue sheet > individual WAVs >
 light editing on some tracks > Flac 1.1.2 with tags > dime & abmsd (April 2005)

I obtained the audio discs directly from the taper, and I edited the track 
breaks plus a couple of other things.  If you want to check this against a 
copy you have, check the audio fingerprints toward the end of In The Garden.  
If you have an original copy, then the left channel will be missing for the 
first 1:43 of Clean-Cut Kid and there will be a 6-second dropout around 1:12 
of Let The Good Times Roll.  See the annotated tracklist below, and the 
HTML tracklist for more detail.

I've included an MD5 file and a Flac fingerprint for the Flac files,
and a log of my EAC extraction (separate files for each disc).

The Extras folder contains, well, a few extras - cue sheets, spectral/frequency
view, md5s for WAV files, HTML tracklist, shntool len output.  
I found no art that matches this version.
