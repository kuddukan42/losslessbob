Bob comment before 'Til I Fell In Love With You:
Thanks everybody. That's not Robbie Robertson up here. I know, some people
asked me. That's Larry Campbell. He's playing the guitar. Robbie couldn't make it. David Kemper he's on the drums. Bucky Baxter, he's the former mayor of Bluesfield, West Virginia. Here in LA for one of the very few times, he's
playing the mandolin and steel guitar. And Tony Garnier, he's playing on the
bass tonight. Thanks a lot.
