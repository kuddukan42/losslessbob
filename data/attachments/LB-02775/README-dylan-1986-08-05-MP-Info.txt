Mountain View, August 5, 1986 - taper markp - 3 discs
Bob Dylan
Shoreline Amphitheatre, Mountain View, CA, August 5, 1986

Source/lineage information: taper markp, transfer from the master cassettes
Nakamichi CM300(cp4) > Sony tcd5m > nakamichi cr-1a > hhb cdr830 >
 EAC > single WAV > track breaks edited via cue sheet > individual WAVs >
 light editing on some tracks > Flac 1.1.2 with tags > dime & abmsd (May 2005)

I obtained the audio discs directly from the taper, and I edited
the track breaks plus a couple of other things.  If you want to
check this against a copy you have, the most prominent fingerprint
is the enthusiastic woman who is heard several times.  Check her out
during Breakdown and Band of the Hand.  Other fingerprints occur during
We Had It All and early in Hard Rain. If you have an original
copy, you have an extra track fragment at the end of discs 1 & 2.

the very start of the first track will be missing, and there
will be a 4-second dropout near the start of Breakdown, from a tape flip.
See the annotated tracklist below, and the HTML tracklist for more detail.

I've included an MD5 file and a Flac fingerprint for the Flac files,
and a log of my EAC extraction (separate files for each disc).

The Extras folder contains cue sheets, spectral/frequency views,
md5s for WAV files, HTML tracklist, shntool len output.
I found no art that matches this version, but I included a ticket scan.
