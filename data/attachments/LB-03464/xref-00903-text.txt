Hi everybody

This is the second one of the "Acid Project" and the first one in "Mind Out Of Time" project.
I like so much what Dylan did in August of 1997, that i have decided to remaster as much concerts from August that i can.If someone can help with uploading August 97 shows,here on HC,i shall remaster them all and put in nice artwork.
This is my second remaster of this show.Before the crash of HC i have uploaded my previous remaster,this one is different.
It is remastered from a different source and is much better IMHO.I have downloaded original files from Dime,some time ago and original info is included in torrent.

My plan is to remaster and to do proper artwork for some bootlegs.Mostly i am gonna work on The Rolling Stones and Bob Dylan.
My general view on remastering these days, is not to touch anything unless i absolutely think it`s necessary.By all means i never-ever use noise reduction (i hate it).I now-days try to avoid compression, but sometimes i have to work with it, and i do it very gently.
Actually my remastering is just to do some EQ, in most of the cases, and bit of level correction and editing.

I gotta tell you that music is my profession and i work in recording studios for more than 31 years now. So i shall try to up the standard for the sound and for the artwork in the bootleg world.
As for the artwork, Mr.D , my good friend, is gonna take care about that.
That is all for now,enjoy.

Captain Acid
October 2011
