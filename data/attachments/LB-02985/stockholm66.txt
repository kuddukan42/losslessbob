Bob Dylan 
Konserthuset
Stockholm, Sweden 
April 29, 1966

-primitive audience recording, but good for the time-

***important*** this is a flawed copy. There are periodic digital glitches
throughout. I am posting it due to it being about 10 times better sounding than
the version of this show that graces the "Jewels & Binoculars" set. 
While that version is glitch free it seems to be a very high 
generation tape compared to this one.

What I am hoping to achieve by posting this is not for tons of people to download it, but maybe a few people will that have the 
capacity and will to fix these glithes and repost for all to enjoy. here's hoping.
