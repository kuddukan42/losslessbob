   This show was at a minor league baseball field.  The Green Cards opened up
before Willie Nelson took the stage.  After Willie's set, Bob came
on and gave us a real fan's setlist.   His new band seems to be more
of a boogie-woogie band than the previous musicians that played with
him on the 2003 tour that I saw.   The songs tend to swing a bit and
are somewhat stretched out.

   The stage was setup at the center field wall and the soundboard tent 
was at the end of the infield. The audiance had free roam of the stadium 
and the outfield, but the infield and dugouts were fenced off.   
  
     I stood about 20 feet to the right of the soundboard.  The sound was
pretty good for an open field, the crowd mostly noticable between songs. 
As always, your results may vary.   

     I had two drop-outs in this recording.  - One, at the :47 sec mark on 
"Love Minus Zero(No Limit)" and one at the 1:44 mark on "The Times
They Are A Changin'".  Both drop-outs were less than a second, so I 
dropped them into Cool-Edit Pro and closed up the gaps. 

    I was snapping pics during Willie's set and got busted with the camera
so there are no pics of Bob contained herein. If anyone was there and 
would like to add their Bob pics, I'll adjust the artwork to include them.
