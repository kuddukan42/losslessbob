Bob Dylan
The Rolling Thunder Review
Complete Show
Maple Leaf Gardens
 
Toronto, Ontario, Canada
 
1 December 1975
 
 This is the complete Rolling Thunder Review show, 4 discs, almost 200 minutes.  Included are sets by Bob Neuwirth, T.Bone Burnett, Ronee Blakely, Joni Mitchell,  Ramblin' Jack Elliot,  Joan Baez,  Roger McGuinn, Gordon Lightfoot,  and others as well as, of course Bob Dylan.
