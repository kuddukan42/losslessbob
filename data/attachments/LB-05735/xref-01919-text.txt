Bob Dylan
Dylan Paso Por... Gijon

Plaza de Toros
Gijon, Spain
July 8, 1993

Audience recording. Same as LB-5735. As the other LB entries for this date has nothing above 16k but no lego parapets.

Taper: LTD
Lineage: Cd-R(x) > wav (eac secure mode) > wav (adobe audition 1.5 / fix) > flac (flac frontend 1.2.1b w/flac frontend 1.7.1 - align on SB option - level 8 - verify on)

This was part of a fan project of unknown origin, a series compiling altogether recordings from several shows of various Spanish tours since 1989, each volume (2cd) including a different show. Received in trade late in 1999 or early 2000. Intro seems to be here a little bit longer than it is on LB-5735. My discs had TAO gaps between tracks. I have repaired most of the digital problems detected by digi-flaw-finder software. Disc 1 last track fade out and d2t01 fade in have been left as they were in the original cd-R's. However the encore was cut and d2t06, Maggie's Farm, had also a fade out that I managed to undo, though variably increasing levels still left minimal drops on the previously fade out segment (dff did only detect 3 of them). d2t05 had a gap at end and last sound before that had a final portion that repeated itself at the beginning of d2t05 which had to be removed. d2t06-07 transition is not seamless, changing now more or less abruptly from one track to the other. I thought I'd rather choose to restore the sound of the "oe-oeing" crowd on last seconds of d2t06 than preserve the fade out to make a smoother transition. Hope that's not a major problem to anyone.    

Enjoy!

Luisbp51
