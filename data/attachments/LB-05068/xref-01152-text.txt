Bob Dylan
Sandstone Ampitheatre, Bonner Springs, KS
2000/07/04

Sony D6 w/ Sonic Studios>Master tapes (Maxell UDXLIIS)>Cool Edit Pro>CDR>EAC>FLAC

From my master tapes. There are several recordings of this concert. Mine is better than others Ive heard for the following reasons: 
I was able to adjust the volume to get the quieter parts of the concert on tape without being too low. I had a seat that was a ways back,
 but not too far, and there were not that many people around me during Dylans opening set, so there is not the annoying chatter found 
on the versions taped closer to the stage. 
  Some unusual set-list choices for this tour. I think it was the only time he played "Born in Time" on the whole tour. There are 2 or 3 
other songs that were only played a very few times this tour as well. 

I don't recall exactly who was in the band besides Bob, but the ususal suspects at that time

Bob Dylan- guitar, vocals, harmonica
Larry Cambell - guitar, fiddle, mandolin, etc.
Tony Garnier- Bass
David Kemper-Drums
Charlie Sexton - guitars 
(Bucky Baxter had left by this point in time and I think Charlie was on board by now)
