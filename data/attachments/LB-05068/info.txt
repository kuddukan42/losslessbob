
Bob Dylan
Sandstone Ampitheatre, Bonner Springs, KS 
July 7, 2000
From Master Tapes

Sony D-6 w/ Sonic Studios>Cool Edit Pro>Master CDR>EAC>Flac 

Dylan opened for Phil Lesh and friends. He started right on time to the minute. Many people were still coming in or not interested in listening, so all recordings of this show have a lot of talking as, even in the front rows, people were just talking and not listening.
This tape is the best I have heard from this show as the talking is generally far in the background. The seats around me were filled with Dylan fans or empty. This was recorded from about 23 or so rows back in the right center front section. I included mp3 samples to let you judge the audio. If you know of a better recording, please upload it. 
I seeded this before the change to EZ Torrent and was told it was the best sounding recording of this show. I've only heard a couple more and both have lots of chatter. 

I don't have all the Bob talk info, but he does tell a really corny joke. I believe that "Born in Time" was only perfomed one other time that year, and no other times on the summer tour. A couple other songs were performed only rarely that year. Enjoy!
