I am posting the original SHN file set for LB-1404.  

Or that was my plan.  

LB-1404 has a bunch of issues though.  It is actually a bit of a mess:  

1.  The headers had extra RIFF chunks.  I stripped all of the headers.

2.  On the LB-1404 setlist you can read this in the second part of the show:
 
Encore
Encore
Encore
Highway 61 Revisited 

Someone turned 3 minutes of applause and crowd noise preceding the first tune in the encore into three tracks (CD 2, tracks 5, 6, and 7) and the beginning of a fourth, Highway 61 Revisited (track 8). I combined the applause and crowd noise preceding Highway 61 Revisited into one track. 

3.  And since my fingers were now into this project, I discovered that invariably applause and crowd noise were at the beginning of each track.  And, you know, to be really honest here, if I want to rehear a song, I always enjoy trudging my way through 90 seconds of applause and whistles before I get to the tune itself.  No, really, I don't.  I split every track and attached the applause and crowd noise portion to the end of the each prior track so each track mark starts with a tune rather than with applause and whistles.  

What I didn't do was alter the audio.  It remains unchanged and if there were problems with it, those remained.  Even the whistlers are still there. Even the annoying ones.           

Bob Dylan
Recreation and Convocation Center (RACC)
State University of New York at Albany (SUNY)
