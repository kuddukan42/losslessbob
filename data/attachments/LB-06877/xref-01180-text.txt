Bob Dylan - Prague March 11, 1995

This version is made from a 6 disc CD-R aud set of unknown generation received from a french trader some years ago - no info on taper or lineage. It was accompanied by a link to the html file attached here describing the contents of the six discs. 
Disc 2 contained 7 bonus tracks - not extracted here. From the html file included it appears to be identical with the 12/A Pound Bootleg.

Disc 1 (72:07)

01 Crash On The Levee (Down In The Flood)
02 If Not For You
03 All Along The Watchtower
04 Just Like A Woman
05 Tangled Up In Blue
06 Watching The River Flow
07 Mr. Tambourine Man
08 Boots Of Spanish Leather
09 It's All Over Now Baby Blue
10 Man In The Long Black Coat
11 God Knows

Disc 2 (17:06)

01 Maggie's Farm
02 Shelter From The Storm
03 It Ain't Me, Babe
