05. Maggie's Farm

07. Isis

09. Railroad Boy
10. I Pity The Poor Immigrant
11. Shelter From The Storm
12. I Threw It All Away
13. Stuck Inside Of Mobile With The Memphis Blues Again
14. One More Cup Of Coffee (Valley Below)
15. Going, Going, Gone
16. Lay, Lady, Lay
17. Silver Mantis
18. Mozambique
19. Idiot Wind
20. Knockin' On Heaven's Door
21. Gotta Travel On


======

1-2 Bob solo acoustic
3 with Bobby Neuwirth
8-10 with Joan Baez
8 - an intruder is on the stage, Bob and Joan call for security to remove him
17 sung by T-bone Burnette
20 with Roger McGuinn
21 ensemble.

======


audiowhore repair project - 1976
source used: LB-4325 (lk: My Low gen Tape > CDR > EAC > Flac)


Notes about this recording:
- all thanks to the taper for being there (a female voice can be heard assisting the taper too)
- stereo recording!!
- pitch was too low and speed too slow
- nothing from this show has been officially released
- tape flips after tracks 7 and 13
- tracks 14 and 17 cut at start
- track 21 cut at 2 minute mark.


What i have done:
- adjust pitch and speed for each song individually to correct
- fix SBEs
- eq mixing to better balance sound
- some hard limit on loud clapping
- tape flips, minor dropouts, microphone bumps cleaned/manually removed
- No Noise or hiss reduction used.


Notes about other versions of this show:
There is only one known source.
LB-3115, LB-3454, LB-4325 and LB-4600 are all derived from the same recording.

========

Rolling Thunder Revue part II ensemble:

Bob Dylan
Rob Stoner (bass)
Scarlet Rivera (violin)
T-bone J. Henry Burnette (guitar & piano)
Steven Soles (guitar)
Mick Ronson (guitar)
Bobby Neuwirth (guitar & vocal)
Roger McGuinn (guitar & vocal)
David Mansfield (steel guitar, mandolin, violin & dobro)
Howie Wyeth (drums)
Gary Burke (congas).

I will keep sharing these on Dime if the following requests are respected:
