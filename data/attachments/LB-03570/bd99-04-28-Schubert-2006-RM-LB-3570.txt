Most songs, however are recorded as good as it gets., In my opinion this new Schubert remaster make the recording sound even better than heard before., No new artwork made yet., Received as flac files directly from Schubert Feb 2006.; excellent sound [A-]; loud songs at end get harsh as if for clipping such as d2t5 2:52 the wav file sort of breaks up (this is also on bootleg Roadmaps to the Soul LB-1262); in comparison to LB-1262 this one is of very similar sound quality
