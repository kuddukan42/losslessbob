Bob Dylan
July 21, 1981
Stadhalle
Vienna, Austria   


alternate recording

This is not LB-192 and not LB-3413. 

Linage: 
mic: AKG D202 - rec: Uher CR 240 (TDK SA90 CC) - 
Akai tape deck - Yamaha CDR-HD1300E - (CDR) - dBpoweramp CD Ripper (secure, flac level 5)


Years ago, I was able to play the original-master-CCs to my HD-Recorder. 
Now, I can share this recording with permission from taper Danneberg himself. Thanks! 

Special thanks also to my friends M. and W. for bringing this recording to the light 
and subsequent digging for (linage-)information.  

This recording "struggles" with a belated start into the first track, 
an several cuts, but it prove to be a very fine audience recording from track 6 on.
