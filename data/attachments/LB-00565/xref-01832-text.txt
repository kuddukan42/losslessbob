Alleged, like LB-0716, to be a partial soundboard recording, missing the first three tunes, LB-0565 is probably not a soundboard recording.  LB-2860, described as an audience recording attributed to Bob Berger, also has the three tunes missing, and is roughly the same as the alleged soundboard (LB-0565, LB-0716)(ref. jokerman.org).
