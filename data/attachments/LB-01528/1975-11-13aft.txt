"We'll Meet Again Someday on the Avenue"
Bob Dylan
1975-11-13 afternoon show
New Haven
Southside Butcher's series


This bit torrent is the Southside Butcher's version of the Dylan set for 11-13-75, 
the afternoon show: "We'll Meet Again Someday on the Avenue". While my experience 
with the Southside Butcher's series is that the recordings are of very good quality,
I have not had a chance to compare this recording with the version that circulated
some time back under the title "The Ratso Disks" (which included both the
afternoon and evening shows). My copy of that recording is on another continent, subject
to neither review nor torrenting. In any case, if you do not have this show in good
quality and are an RTR fan (hard to imagine that there could be anyone who is not!), 
then grab this.
