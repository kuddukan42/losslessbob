Bob Dylan at the Warfield
2 Nov 1979

A stereo audience recording of the 2nd show of this brand new and most surprising tour.
This transfer was never seeded before- the recording itself was probably never seeded before.

This is a follow-up to the more complete (but muffled) composite version ("V3") seeded a few days ago by Chuck K.  The non-Dylan performances are not here.

Jeff Friedman made the cleanest recording ("V1"), but this taper had a better seat though there are problems with a bad connection and some slight tape fading.
