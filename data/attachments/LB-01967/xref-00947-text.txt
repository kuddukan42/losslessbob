BOB DYLAN
The Mystery Tape

Excellent quality
Thanks to Hanspeter Speiss who provided me with this long-seeked piece of music

1. Sally Gal - take 4
2. Ramblin Down Through the World - 12 April 63
3. Hiding too Long - 12 April 63
4. Suze (Cough Song) - with intro
5. You Don't Have To Do That - 13 Jan 65
6. Jet Pilot
7. Working On A Guru
8. Let Me See
9. Dark Groove - Infidels outtake
10. Don't Fly Unless It's Safe - Infidels outtake
11. Neighbourhood Bully - Infidels outtake
12. Silvio - rough mix
13. Dirty World - rough mix
14. Ring Them Bells - Oh Mercy outtake
15. Dicease of Conceit - Oh Mercy outtake
16. What Was It You Wanted - Oh Mercy outtake
17. 4th Time Around - Blonde On Blonde remix
18. Sooner or Later - unfaded 45
19. Visions of Johanna - Blonde On Blonde remix

In 1992 the 'Mystery Tape' emerged as nice little item and quite a few of the tracks  
were totally new to collectors. Possibly, this tape was put together for the purposes 
of copywriting this material. Here it is; quite a diverse but unique tape.
The three Blonde On Blonde bonus tracks are meant to be separate tracks and all start 
and end in either total or near-total silence.
