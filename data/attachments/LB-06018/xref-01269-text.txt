Bob Dylan: We Had Known a Lion

Dylan bootleg released in 1998 on the Vigotone label. Contains the September 3 1965 show at the Hollywood Bowl, Hollywood, CA. It has three extra tracks not present on the Electric Black Nite Crash bootleg, and a booklet with the concert review. The electric set featured Robbie Robertson (guitar), Levon Helm (drums), Al Kooper (organ) and Harvey Brooks (bass.). This was the second Dylan concert with members of The Hawks.
