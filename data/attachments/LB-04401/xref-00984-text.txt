Hi Dylan fanatics,
this is my second remaster of Dylan, in Acid Project series. With this one i had much help from Erik Snow, he helped me track the best source for the remaster and even provided the photo for Mr D to work with.
What i did here is some phase correction, level correction and the most important EQ. 
I do it with the "Waves" plug ins in Soundforge 10.0 Pro.
I have also reconstructed the missing part in the announcement of the gig. I took the missing part from another source and edited it.
No noise reduction, compression or limiting were used.
Mister D made another wonderful cover art.

Enjoy

Captain Acid    February 2012
