Seattle, WashingtonSeattle CenterKey ArenaOctober 4, 2002The first of the piano shows! I received this as a cdr from a trader not long after the performance with no recording information.    Audio was extracted with Max v0.7.1 (w/ full paranoia) to flac level 8.  I don't know if this recording has been given an LB number.  Judging by the times for each disk, it is not LB-692...  And the times are close, but not exactly the same as, those listed for LB-1248 (off by 53 seconds for disk one and by 20 seconds for disk two), so this could be a new recording.  I haven't got either LB-692 or LB-1248 to directly compare...  Artwork is included.This is a near excellent recording.  Enjoy.Uploaded to Hungercity:  May 2007.Disk One  (69:03)   1.
