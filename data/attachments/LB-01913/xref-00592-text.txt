Review: 
This set represents a good quality soundboard tape of the Denmark show.  
Along with the 7-8 star rating all one had to judge the set by, it seems most desirable. 
However, consider the following. The Denmark show is not only unremarkable, but some songs teeter on the brink of disaster. 
The band sometimes falters, Dylan's phrasing and pitch sometimes float around into other worlds (the lifeless, and uninhabited ones); 
the guitar is a tad out of tune, and even though Dylan is talkative; the general feeling seems to be "Let's get this job over with" 
Even though the tape is soundboard with audience buried far to the background, the tape is muffled somewhat
