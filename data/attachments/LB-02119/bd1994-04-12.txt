Tale of the Tape:
This analog came from the estate of Guy Gimbrone (RIP) of Roscoe, IL.  This tape was not 
labelled as first gen. but I am calling it so for a few reasons.  First of all, there is  
very little hiss to the recording.  Secondly, the way that Watching the River Flow was allowed
to cut and the fact that side B came in with crowd right away leads me to believe that the 
taper just ran this straight from his DAT and and didn't bother to try and cut the tape in 
such a way as to get a complete show.  Thirdly, I remember Guy saying that he sat right by the
taper, and they talked about the rig being run (Guy was a taper himself) and the taper 
promised to send Guy a copy, so I know it's direct from the taper himself.  It seems that the
taper did not bother to put the encores on a seperate tape as this tape was not labelled as "1"
or anything, so until a complete copy emerges, this is all we have.
I remember Guy telling me about the person you will hear prior to Series of Dreams (which I 
believe is it's last known performance as of 10/24/04), and I think that may be Guy you hear
chuckling as the person freaks out.
This was also the show from which Bob donated $20,000 to Page School in Rockford to help them
rebuild their playground, Bob mentions this before the band intros.  Apparently BOb read about
the lack of funds in the paper and decided unbeknownst to anyone to donate the money.  The 
check was received by Federal Express a few days after the performance.

This seed dedicated to the memory of Guy Gimbrone, I miss you meatball.
