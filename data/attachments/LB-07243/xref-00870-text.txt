DYLAN and DEAD
The Complete Rehearsals  (So Far ... )
recorded live at the Club Front : San Rafael : California, June 1987

These are the Audio CDR's  received from the tree offered at Dylantree

Lineage: Tree Audio CDR'S ->  EAC (secure/offset correct)  ->  WAV  ->  TLH - FLAC Level 8

> Original Artwork Included 
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

All Information below is borrowed from, LosslessBob LB-7243 info file

This 6-cd set, donated by Alan Fraser, documents all the known Dylan and Dead rehearsals from the Club Front in San Rafael, CA (June 1987) in incredible sound quality. All the songs come from soundboard cassette masters transferred to DAT, then CD-R.

The layout of the set is as follows:

Disc #1 - Prehearsals
Discs #2,3,4,5 - Main rehearsals
Disc #6 - The Grateful Dead Hour #705, broadcast week of 25 Mar 2002 

Extra Information
This set could be broken up into three different trees: prerehearsals, rehearsals, and the grateful dead hour #705. However, I have decided to keep them together in one tree for the sake of completeness and ease.
