Bob Dylan And His Band - November 18 - 2004
Whittemore Center Arena, University Of New Hampshire, Durham NH


Received by trading some time ago three gens from taper. Sounding better than the "Streetcar Visons" and the "Unwanted Man Records" versions. Not compared to the "Soomlos" recording yet. my traded copy had gaps and glitches and some disturbing boosting of some tracks.
Fixed, and now sounding excellent - and almost flawless - and added artwork made by Ken(Scouserart) for this "remaster" - prepared for dylanvine.
Same original source have been vined on dylantree recently as Durham 2004 Special Edition.

I nice performance by Dylan, and band very tight.

Concert # 1697 of The Never-Ending Tour. Concert # 27 of the 2004 US Fall College Tour. 2004 concert # 109. 
Concert # 76 with the 18th Never-Ending Tour Band: Bob Dylan (vocal,harmonica & piano), Stu Kimball (guitar),  Larry Campbell (guitar, mandolin, pedal steel guitar & electric slide guitar), Tony Garnier (bass), George Receli (drums & percussion).
