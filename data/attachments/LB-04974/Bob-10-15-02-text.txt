Bob Dylan
The Wiltern
Los Angeles, CA
October 15, 2002

Great show.  Great recording.  This is the first night of the three Wiltern shows in October 2002.  Taped from third row in the Loge with the same gear as my 2006 LA Forum show, and it sounds very similar, maybe a bit better. There may be other versions of this show, but this one has never been circulated and is certainly worth having.  Very clear, even mix.  Guitars, piano and vocals very clear.  A good crowd.  Very little close clapping and no screamers.

Didn't know how busy I would at work, so I initially only bought tickets for the first night.  Lots of tickets out front before the show.  When the show was so great, I went back for night three figuring I could easily snag a ticket.  After night one, the LA Times printed a glowing review, including the printing of the setlist.  There wasn't an extra ticket in sight.
