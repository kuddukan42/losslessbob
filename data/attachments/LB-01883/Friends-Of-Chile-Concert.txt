FELT FORUM, MADISON SQUARE GARDEN
NEW YORK CITY, NEW YORK

FRIENDS OF CHILE CONCERT

1 - Deportee (Plane Wreck At Los Gatos)(W Guthrie/M Hoffman)
2 - North Country Blues
3 -  comments
4 - Spanish Is The Loving Tongue (C B Clark/J Williams)
5 - Blowin' In The Wind

Arlo Guthrie	Guitars

Dave Van Ronk	Guitars
Larry Estridge	
Melanie	Guitars
Pete Seeger	Guitars
Phil Ochs	Guitars


Concert organised by Phil Ochs. Track 1 Arlo lead vocal.  The show included speeches by Larry Estridge on Chile. Chile. Dylan appeared as a favour to Phil  Ochs. Due to some early back stage drinking Bob's pretty drunk at the start, giving a very shakey performance. Some press reports unkindly suggesting that Bob falls off the stage at the end !
