Bob Dylan
Presumably recorded on a street outside of
Rundown Studios, Santa Monica, CA, January 1978.

The "Street Tape" is Disc 3 of the
Complete 1978 Tour Rehearsals, a fan project.
[The other 3 discs are superseded by the White Bear
release "The Rundown Rehearsal Tapes"]
Probably the same as http://www.angelfire.com/wa/monicasdude/missreh.htm

I have no source/lineage information for this disc;
I don't even remember how I got it.  But the quality
is so bad, we don't have to worry about such things!
