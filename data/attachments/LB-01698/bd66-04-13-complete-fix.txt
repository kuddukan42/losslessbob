Bob Dylan, 4/13/66, Sydney Stadium, Sydney, New South Wales, Australia, 2CDR

(49min+48min), Complete show, SBD recording, "off master", Sydney was the first show of the 1966 World Tour outside the USA. This recording contains the full intermission and, "tuning up" before the electric set and is also notable for the rare electric "Positively Fourth Street."; reported as an upgrade;; excellent sound; this version turns out to be the editted version; also received an optional fix for d1t1 (3:52) and d1t7 (25:17) which enabled this to be the real complete version - d1t1 is a little longer version of she belongs to me., d1t7 is tambourine man, PLUS the intermission.

some mic hits and wind type noises occasional throughout; small analog drop d1t5 5:34, 5:42, analog static d1t5 9:44, long version of d1t1 has abrupt start with analog drops at 0:11; long version of d1t7 has large digi-spikes at 25:17 at very end of  track
