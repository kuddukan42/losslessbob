Bob Dylan 
13 April 1966 
Sydney Stadium 
Sydney, New South Wales, Australia
Complete show * 
SBD recording, "off master" 

Sydney was the first show of the 1966 World Tour outside the USA.  This recording contains the full intermission and 
"tuning up" before the electric set and is also notable for the rare electric "Positively Fourth Street."
