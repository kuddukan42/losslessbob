Bob Dylan
At The Beeb
[Mainstream MSBR-039]

BBC Studios, BBC broadcast session, London, June 1, 1965
BBC broadcast in two parts on BBC radio, June 19 & 26, 1965
No source/lineage information
Remastered by Improved Air Remasters, April, 2003

broadcast part 1, June 19, 1965:
1. Ballad Of Hollis Brown
2. Mr Tambourine Man 
3. Gates Of Eden
4. If You Gotta Go Go Now 
5. Lonesome Death Of Hattie Carroll 
6. It Ain't Me Babe

broadcast part 2, June 26, 1965:
7. Annoucement / Love Minus Zero/No Limit 
8. One Too Many Mornings 
9. Boots Of Spanish Leather 
10. It's Alright Ma (I'm Only Bleeding) 
11. She Belongs To Me
12. It's All Over Now Baby Blue 

NOTE: While this disc is an upgrade over the commonly circulating
commercial boots and their clones, it is definitely not taken from
the BBC tapes which turned up in private hands in 2002. Those tapes
have not been circulated.
