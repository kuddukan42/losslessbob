Review (http://www.bobsboots.com/CdS/cd-c23.html)
The powerful Warfield concert begins on this disc and is concluded on the companion disc "Part 2".  This near flawless audience recording preserves it in all its glory. This gospel show explodes with vocal passion to be found in few other Dylan periods. If you're a fan of Dylan's gospel period, this set is a 'Must-have'.
