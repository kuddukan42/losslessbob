Solomons, Maryland
Calvert Marine Museum
May 11, 2003

This is an outdoor recording of a very good concert, Bob and his Band on the dock playing great Rock and Roll underneath the light of the silvery moon.  Expect to hear some wind noises on this recording, this was recorded outdoors with the wind blowing in off the Chesapeake Bay by our man wearing a hat.  This is the next best thing to 'being there' , better than that.  Thank you, Jeff F.
