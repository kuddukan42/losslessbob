Thank you very much ladies and gentlemen for coming out tonight. I wanna introduce these young ladies to you. On my left

Portsmouth, Virginia, William Smith. On guitar from Paris, France, Fred Tackett. From Copenhagen on guitar Steve Ripley.

Drummond. He's got one of the last bass guitars around that's why. On the drums, from Tulsa, Oklahoma, Jim Keltner. I
hope we played some thing that you came to hear tonight. I know we left a lot of songs out that I wanted to play. Maybe next
time now, good night. (at the end of In The Garden)
