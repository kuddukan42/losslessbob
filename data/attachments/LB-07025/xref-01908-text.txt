The Gorge Amphitheatre
George, Washington
June 13, 1999

NOTE: Paul Simon was the opening act

As I received this recording in a snail mail trade with CB in May 2005, 12 yrs. b4 the JEMS Archive recording (presumably) surfaced in June 2017, I might have had my hands on the JEMS at an earlier date; claim supported by the fact that, at the end of 04 'Knockin' on heaven's door' (duet w Paul Simon), one of the JEMS crew shouts More! several times (as mentioned in both LB-12718 and LB-12479). Dylans set I cannot identify  for sure.

An excellent recording!
