John Leiper has recently vined the LJK '1984 PA collection'. 
He asked me about it and I realised this old version should be expanded and improved.

Recently I took the time to sort out the mess and to finish off this project. 
So here it is: fully revised & 'complete'.It has been expanded from 2 CDs to about 5-6 CDs (DVD). 
Rome, Paris, Slane & Wembley are added to and the quality is upgraded where possible.
There are now even a few additional 'PA' tracks that are not annotated in 'Tangled - GlenD'.
If you had the old version from a few years ago, you may wish to upgrade to this one.
