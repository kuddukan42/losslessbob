Bob Dylan
October 18th, 2006
Arco Arena, Sacramento, CA

Bob>Core Directionals>Sony MZR900>Cool Edit>WAV>Flac

Recorded by mike_markov@yahoo.com

Recorded 8th row to the just right of center stage. This is for my dear friend Henry.

I always seem to have trouble taping in this arena, the sound in the arena is just terrible. The only thing
that made up for it was one sensational performance from Bob. I'll post samples so you can be the judge.
I rather like it myself, I actually like it a lot. I'm listening to the burned cds from these flac files
now.

There are some weird digital "noises" in this periodically, I didn't even catch them until I had put on headphones. 
There were so many people playing with cell phones that I think they were causing interference. The start of
"Watchtower" has some nasty interference as does Nettie Moore (for a few seconds) and LARS (a couple of times). Hopefully there will be 
a different source of this. 
I'm posting this because no one else is posting the new shows and I know some people want all the sources. Plus I know of a few people that
want to hear the new material live (First time Nettie Moore here!)

Other notes:

Kings of Leon opened and were fantastic. I'll torrent that show next if someone can help
with a setlist. I have both the albums but I'm just terrible with song titles.

The boos you hear in "Its Alright Ma" are in response to a taper in the 6th row getting snagged by some over zealous 
security guards. There's nothing about no taping on the tickets (as there used to be), just no 
cameras stressed throughout the venue. He came back unscathed fortunately. They even gave him a rubber glove as a
souvenier (just kidding about that part :>)
