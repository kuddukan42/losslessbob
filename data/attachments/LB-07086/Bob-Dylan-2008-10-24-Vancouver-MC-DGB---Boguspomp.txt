Notes by DGB :

Recorded 1st block left hand side of the stage. Incredible sound from where
I was sitting. The side speakers were facing right at me. Probably the best
sound I have ever experienced for a concert at GM Place. Bob put on a great
performance. He really seemed as if he was enjoying himself. One of the best
Bob Dylan shows that I have seen in the last few years. I am very pleased
with the way my recording came out! 

Notes by Boguspomp :

Another very nice recording by DGB. Some fine tuning and Volume adjustment using SF 9 with Waves Platinum Tools.

This is Kick Ass Recording KAR-004
