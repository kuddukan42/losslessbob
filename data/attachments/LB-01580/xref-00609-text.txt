Legendary Tapers Series #12 (Taper=LTB)
Bob Dylan
1990-01-30 Paris
Off-master of a great audience recording by Legendary Taper B

This recording is one of the recordings that LTB made of the London-Paris shows in
1990. In my opinion, these recordings of the London-Paris residency are fabulous.
There is also a very fine set of Holy Grail recordings for the 1990 London-Paris shows. I 
have no information as to the source of these HG recordings, and have not done any detective
work to see if there is any relationship between the source tape for the Holy Grail 
recordings and the LTB recordings. From what knowledge I have of the Holy Grail recordings,
I expect they have a different source.
