Bob Dylan with Tom Petty And The Heartbreakers & Roger McGuinn
Temples In Flames 1987 Tour
Frankenhalle, Nuremberg - West Germany
16 September 1987

IMPROVED AUDIO

Temples in Flames Tour was a concert tour by Bob Dylan with the support of Tom Petty and the Heartbreakers
and Roger McGuinn.
The tour took place in September - October 1987 in several european cities, it started with 2 shows in Israel
and ended with 4 final dates in London, England.
It consisted of 3 concerts, where Roger McGuinn was the first one, followed by Tom Petty and the Heartbreakers,
before Dylan came in.
It was the last time that Dylan toured with Tom Petty (they played together again just in a couple of shows
many years later on August 9 and 10, 2003 at Holmdel PNC Bank Arts Center).

A lot of detailed info about this and others Dylan's
recordings can be found at this great website:

http_jokerman.org.uk/lb/detail/LBM-year-detail-87.html
http_jokerman.org.uk/

(please, replace _ with :// )


I've received this recording many years ago in a tape trade. Listening it, I have thought to make an upgrade
version seen that my copy was too noised and range of frequencies very low.
So I removed as much noise as possible, rebalanced the sound obtaining a many more clear voice and hightest
frequencies in most of the concert, lastly I have correcting the playing speed which was originally too fast.
Ok, I hope you all may enjoy this reworked version and as usual all the best until the next to everybody!
