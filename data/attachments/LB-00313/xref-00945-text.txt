Not sure where I got this from.  It doesn't have an LB number.  It came bundled with a shn copy of 1989-11-13 Miami and both shows together were given the title The Harder They Fall although as one is encoded in flac and the other in shn it seems highly unlikely that they started life as a unit.

Under the notes on LosslessBob for LB-313 is the following comment


(a bittorrent from 02/09 is close eac match on d1t2 with same flaws; described as "Received on trade data DVD, torrented on TangledUpIn Torrents February 11, 2009 by g, r;"; ) tracked with first track broken into 2 tracks and with filenames like "CD1\02 The harder they come.flac")

As the file names are very similar to those noted at LosslessBob and the first track is divided into two this could well derive from LB-313.

Anyway wherever it comes from enjoy :-)
