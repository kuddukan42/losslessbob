Comments form Monicasdude's site:
DEEP: The complete Applied Materials arena gig presented in crisp, clear and upfront quality.
A fine recording, with distinct instrumentation and separation, excellent balance and Bob's vocals 
riding nicely in front of it all. Very energetic and inspired performance, 
with strong and charismatic vocals by Bob. The band senses the energy and provides strong, muscular backing,
to boot. They all sound like they are having a ball, which makes for some great listening.
The bonus tracks were thoughtfully selected, a notch below the San Jose quality-wise but still very good. 
Highly recommended.
