Salem, December 2, 1980
Bob Dylan
The Armory, Salem, OR, December 2, 1980

I downloaded this from alt.binaries.music.shn.dylan in March 2003
(posted by RobertOnThis), and then edited it (mostly track break changes),
and converted it to Flac 1.1.2 with simple tags.  I no longer have the originals.

This post includes MD5 files, Flac fingerprints, Cue sheets, 
frequency & spectral views, shntool len output, and an HTML tracklist.
Also included is the original MD5 file, called BD80-12-02-orig-invalid.md5

PAR2 files are included for the Flac files only.

See the INFO file and the HTML tracklist for a few more details.
