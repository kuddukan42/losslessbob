Forum, Inglewood, California, 14th Feb 1974 (Evening)

Track 0 is an informal recording of unknown lineage of conversation between Dylan, Jack Elliott, and two unreliably identified individuals, made backstage between the day's two concerts, including Dylan singing a few lines from "Don't Think Twice."  It closes with Bill Graham's stage announcement at the beginning of the evening concert.
