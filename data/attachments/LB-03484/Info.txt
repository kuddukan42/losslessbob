Bob Dylan, Orlando (Florida) Sports Stadium, 23April76
Mono audience recording

This is a fun show. The recording is average for the time. The taper's seat could have been better.

First-time seed of this transfer from the Lowgen Archives -- Dime, Jan 2006

This tape was reeled in from Bob H's original cassettes (I think). No EQ was applied except for a notch at 2kHz to ease an annoying mic peak. Use your tone controls for custom adjustments, of course.

Please do not reseed elsewhere without this info file and all relevant comments.
