Dylan's first ever visit to Norway, which wasn't too appreciated by the audience. The show started off with several Gospels by the band and the girls before Dylan came on stage. This was the first performance of Jesus Is The One which he apparently wrote in Oslo (according to Olof). It doesn't sound very rehearsed to be honest... A few bad edits between the songs in the encors, otherwise v sound-wise. Sounds like a soundboard, but some occasional audience-chatter indicates an audience recording.
