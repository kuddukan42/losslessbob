I played at the Frank Sinatra Tribute show a few years back, and I played this next song. We had it all worked out and everything, but then they said they wanted to hear this one instead so ... Restless Farewell was dedicated to Frank Sinatra who passed away the day before this show. The second known live rendition of Restless Farewell, the first being performed at the Frank Sinatra 80th birthday in Los Angeles November 19, 1995. There is also a rumoured live version from Royal Festival Hall in London May 17, 1964
