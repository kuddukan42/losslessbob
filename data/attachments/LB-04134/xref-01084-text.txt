Pavilho do Atlntico
Parque das Naes
Lisbon, Portugal
7 April 1999

This is the Schubert remaster of this 2 shows 
No further information on taper and equipment. 


1. Roving Gambler (trad.)
2. Mr. Tambourine Man
3. Masters Of War
4. Girl From The North Country
5. Tangled Up In Blue
6. Tryin' To Get To Heaven
7. Maggie's Farm
8. Lay Lady Lay
9. Blind Willie McTell
10. Just Like A Woman
11. Can't Wait
12. Like A Rolling Stone

13. Love Sick
14. Leopard-Skin Pill-Box Hat
15. Blowin' In The Wind
16. Highway 61 Revisited

Notes.
Bob Dylan played acoustic guitar on the live debut of Tryin' To Get To Heaven.
There was a 15 minute break before Maggie's Farm.
Stereo audience recording, 105 minutes.
Session info updated 13 January 2000.

Colisio Oporto
Oporto, Portugal
8 April 1999

1. You're Gonna Quit Me (Mance Lipscomb)
2. Mr. Tambourine Man
3. Mama, You Been On My Mind
4. Masters Of War
5. Fourth Time Around
6. Tangled Up In Blue
7. Watching The River Flow
8. Just Like A Woman
9. Tough Mama
10. Simple Twist Of Fate
11. Stuck Inside Of Mobile With The Memphis Blues Again
12. Make You Feel My Love
13. Highway 61 Revisited

14. Love Sick
15. Leopard-Skin Pill-Box Hat
16. Don't Think Twice, It's All Right
17. Like A Rolling Stone

Bob Dylan (vocal & guitar), 
Bucky Baxter (pedal steel guitar & electric slide guitar), 
Larry Campbell (guitar), 
Tony Garnier (bass), 
David Kemper (drums & percussion)

16, 16 acoustic with the band.

BobTalk:
I know we try to play a little bit of everything, and I know people want to hear this and that, but we can't play it all.
Oh, we are gonna play it all. Our bass player's gonna play it all! (during band intro after Make You Feel My Love).

Note. First version of You're Gonna Quit Me since Mountain View 
9 October 1993.
9 new songs (52%) compared to previous concert. 9 new songs for this tour.
Stereo audience recording, 110 minutes.
Session info updated 13 January 2000.
