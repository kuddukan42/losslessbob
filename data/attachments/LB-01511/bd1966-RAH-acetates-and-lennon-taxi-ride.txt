NOTES: 
SOUNDBOARD recording
Recording as complete and in as high grade as known to circulate. 

CDG: These acetates are a considerable upgrade on the previous versions that circulated generally, 
which are thought to have been no better than 2nd analog gen. They are not perfect; there are a number 
of skips and crackles from the original source, although these have been minimised by the CD-R producer. 
They reportedly were mastered from an analog tape taken directly from the original acetates.

The duplication of "Baby Blue" and "Johanna" on Disc ONE has not been explained, and the tracks are not identical.
