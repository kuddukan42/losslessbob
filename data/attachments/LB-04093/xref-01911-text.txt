Another real Dylan gem for you Bob fans. 
I got this from a Dime upload in about 2007, I think. 
Discovered a flaw in track split between d1t4 and d1t5.

So I decoded all flacs to wav and, using wave studio, 
made my own repair of both tracks by cutting the end 
portion of D1T4 and pasting it to the front of D1T5, 
to create d1t4-fixed and d1t5-fixed, for my own listening 
pleasure.

For this offering, I have re-encoded all files to flac8, 
and I am including the original 2 flawed tracks as well as 
my fixed versions of those two, in case you want to make 
your own repair of the two original ones.

Not sure which version of this Bach recording I received, 
but to my ears it sounds like the superior one with full 
spectrum sound. Right channel could stand a small level boost.

I am including a sample for your ears to decide.
