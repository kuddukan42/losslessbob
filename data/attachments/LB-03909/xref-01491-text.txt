BOB DYLAN
9:30 Club, Washington, DC
December 5, 1997

Second night of famous two-night residency at the tiny 9:30 Club in Washington.  Excellent
sound, especially for this venue   a very crowded, very loud venue that is especially difficult to
tape at, and on a night of very high security at the single club entrance.  Great performance too,
you can feel the energy of the venue and its effect on the band.  Four songs from the still new
Time Out Of Mind, and two from the Stanley Brothers.


Disc One
1. Maggie's Farm 
2. I'll Not Be A Stranger (Trad., from the Stanley Brothers)
3. Cold Irons Bound 
4. Just Like A Woman 
5. Can't Wait 
6. Silvio 
7. Roving Gambler (Trad., arr. Dylan)
8. Mr. Tambourine Man

Disc Two
1. Tangled Up In Blue
2. Blind Willie McTell 
3. White Dove (Carter Stanley)
4. 'Til I Fell In Love With You 
5. Highway 61 Revisited 
----
6. Like A Rolling Stone 
7. Don't Think Twice, It's All Right
8. Love Sick 
9. Rainy Day Women #12 & 35
