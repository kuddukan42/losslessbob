A few weeks ago I offered a master from the previous night to this one.
This one I received shortly after the show, but I forget who I received this from (sorry).
This is from the master but I do not remember anything else. I'd like to find out what mic source this may be, & any other info if available.
Jorma Kaukonen does guest on some tracks, & he is on Dime's NAB list, but he's only guesting (not his show), & they do not perform any of his songs.
Larry Campbell, Warren Haynes, & Jorma Kaukonen all bending strings at the same time, nice!
A few nights prior to this Derek Trucks was still in Phil's band, but had to leave, so Jorma was joining. There were a couple shows of Jorma, Derek, & Warren - wow.
Let's not forget this show, as Phil guests with Bob too! This also has SIX encore tracks!
Enjoy!!
