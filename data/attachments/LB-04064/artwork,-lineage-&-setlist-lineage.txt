Summary of restoration.

disc one

T01: Clicks have been removed at 0:03.88, 0:04.29, 0:04.70 & at 0:08.98. The intro was originally part of "Wait For The Light To Shine", it now stands alone as T1, all subsequent tracks having been renumbered. This why the EAC extract log only shows ten tracks on disc one.

T02: A click has been removed at 0:23:15.

T03: This is perfect.

T04: Bob's introduction to Desolation Row has been slightly enhanced whilst a nearby screamer has been toned down. 
A click has been removed at 4:31.85, right in the middle of the word "punished". 
Further clicks have been removed at 5:37.12, 5:39.76, 6:27.77, 6:29.88, 6:32.54, 6:37.59, 6:42.56, 6:44.15, 
6:44.57, 7:25.57, 8:11.72, & 8:12.5. A little adjustment was undertaken over the T4/T5 changeover.

T05: This is perfect

T06: This track has been enhanced by +2dB.

T07: T7 has also been enhanced by +2dB. Clicks at 6:12.97 & 6:13.87 have been removed.

T08: A click at 5:42.09 has been removed, and the track has been enhanced by +2dB.

T09: There are some mild mike bumps at the beginning of this track, these remain. Clicks have been removed at 0:05.37, 0:11.13, & 1:50.88. Some rather staccato claps at the end of the track have been toned down a little. (Gotta say I 
just love this song!) The track has been enhanced by +2dB from 0:02.754 to 1:11.109.

T10: The audience is a little intrusive at the beginning of this track, but things improve as it goes on. At 1:50.92 
a "tsch" has been removed, as at 1:55.76. Clicks at 2:02.44 and 2:02.66 have been removed. The song has been enhanced 
very slightly.

T11: The track changeoever has been modified slightly. The song has been enhanced by +4dB. A big click at 2:22.836 
has been removed, evidence of the edit is regretfully apparent, it could not be avoided. The fade out was followed 
by far too many seconds of silent space, this has been removed.


disc two

T01: Small clicks have been removed at 0:01.55 & 0:02.05, as has a bump at 0:09.76. A non-applause click 
at 8:22.236 and a "pshht" at 8:30.45 have also been removed. This track has been enhanced overall by +4dB. 

T02: Some very faint but audible ticks have been removed around the track changeover. The very beginning of the song 
has been slightly enhanced. A slight crackle has been corrected at 2:27.02. Clicks have been removed at 5:24.91, 5:34.99 
and 5:35.25. There are a few mild mike bumps at the end of the track, these remain. 

T03: I have subjected this track to an enhancement of +8dB, not a huge volume increase at the prevailing amplitude;
prior to this the song was virtually inaudible. The recording is of good quality, and very little noise is apparent. 
Ticks have been removed at 0:00.817, 0:00.365, 0:02.80 & 0:03.184. The track changeover from T2 has been equalised.
At about 1:30 a little squeaking (on the guitar fret?) is apparent. (Perhaps the stage flooring was the guilty party!)
Clicks have been removed at 6:40.709 & 7:00.444

T04: A big click has been removed at 2:30.700. Very light amplitude clipping was apparent in this track during the 
reprise for each verse, and the song finale. This has been corrected. 

T05: A click has been removed at 2:16.90. Some slight amplitude clipping has been corrected throughout this track, and 
some very localised and specific adjustments have been made to the crowd noise at the end.

T06: This is perfect.

T07: A little amplitude clipping has been corrected.

T08: I have enhanced this track by +4dB (again only a slight volume increase at the prevailing amplitude). Sounds better
as a follow on to the previous track. (This also suits the next track.)

T09: The track changeover from T8 to T9 has been slightly modified. A small "tssk" at 0:01.886 has been removed. Most of 
this track has been adjusted in amplitude by -0.5dB to correct some mild clipping.
 
T10: This is perfect
