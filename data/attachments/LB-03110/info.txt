Review: 
This disc presents the Hartford show in fantastic quality. The music and vocals are crisp, powerful, and well mixed. The audience noise is virtually non-existent. The show is great, and chock-full o' rarities. The filler includes the non duplicated songs from the previous night. The package aesthetics are only fair (green and red type doesn't blend well, hand cursive is never a good idea, the title is listed incorrectly on the front, etc.). However, it is offset with some great looking photos that make it worthy to house the CDs. Overall, this is a great set to represent the 1997 spring tour.
