Great performance from St Paul 1978, including the only performance of "Coming From The Heart" Bob ever did in concert.

I have to excuse myself for buying this bootleg, as it's just a copy of a torrent/master which was shared kindly via torrent last year. "Thinman" always copy torrents - and release them as pressed CDs. But I just couldn't help myself from buying this one - to listen to it today, friday afternoon. Great great listen - and I missed last years torrent of it. 
By looking at losslessbob - I assume that this is LB-4798. Many many thanks to the original uploader of that one - and please excuse us who are desperate enough to pay money for such a great free share, to begin with.
(Obviously, I can't tell if "Thinman" made any changes to the sound/mix)

Artwork/scans included - and I took the liberty of changing the title on back/front/discs from "Coming From The Heat Of St Paul" to "Coming From The Heart Of St Paul" - which I suppose the title was meant to be.
No loss in quality while making these changes. Satisfied with the result...it took an hour.
So....if someone is considering buying this Thinman bootleg, then please don't do so - and download this instead - where the title is correct. Instead...you could make a donation to Dimeadozen or Hungercity....2 great resources for all of us. Or....support Bob, by buying the Bootleg Series Volume 8 - which will be out on Oct. 7th 2008.

Enjoy....Erik_Snow
