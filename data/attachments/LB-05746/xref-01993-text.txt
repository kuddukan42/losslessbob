Bob Dylan - Outtakes Of Mercy
Oh Mercy outtakes
Studio On The Move, New Orleans, LO
March-July 1989

Hi Dylan gang.
A friend suggested i might do this, so i did it. I worked on Deeds Of Mercy bootleg. I also had Ring Them Bells and Mercy On Us, those two were already remastered so i took Deeds Of Mercy because it was closest to original, raw files.
I have done speed-pitch, phase and level correction and some gentle EQ. I think this is the first time those outtakes run at correct speed, at least considering commertial bootlegs.
As usual, no compression, limiting or noise reduction was done by me.


Artwork by Mr. D
remastered by Captain Acid December 2019
