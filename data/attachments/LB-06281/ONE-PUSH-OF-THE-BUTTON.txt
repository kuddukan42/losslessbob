ONE PUSH OF THE BUTTON
A new two-disc set from the Hollow Horn label that will form the third volume of the current “Reference Recording” series. This set concentrates on TV performances in the 1963-1969 period (plus one from 1980!). Expect the usual high quality fold out packaging and best available sound quality!
