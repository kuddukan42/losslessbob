Taped by Legendary Taper E.

Watt Alexander in the "Gospel Project" cites two "source recordings" (as before, he guesses
they may come from the same original). The first includes all the girls' songs, but is 
incomplete with respect to the Dylan tracks. The second lacks all but one of the girls' 
songs, but is complete with respect to Dylan's songs. The offmaster recording here is 
the second recording that the "Gospel Project" cites. This recording has some defects: 
"There is a brief drop-out during "Do Right" and some obtrusive audience chatter during 
"Saving Grace," Sadly, the quality of the recording suffers a great deal of fluctuation 
beginning with "Solid Rock" and marring every song thereafter." Thus while the performances
for this show are of outstanding quality, the recording is not without problems.
