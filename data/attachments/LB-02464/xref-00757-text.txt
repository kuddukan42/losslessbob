10.11.1989, Atlanta, 2CDs

LB-2464

cut at the start of d2t6

Another terrific show, although the sound is not the best. The acoustic set (esp. "Dark As A Dungeon") is wonderful & the encores are for me among Dylan's best performances of that year. I love the loud opening chords to "All Along The Watchtower".
