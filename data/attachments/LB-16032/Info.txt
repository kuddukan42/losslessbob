Bob Dylan 
San Jose, CA
12/12/65
2024 Remix

Original Source: LB-12823 (Ginsberg master tape > Flac)

Recording by Allen Ginsberg - December 1965. 
Remixed by Bob Meyer - April 2024.

REMIX NOTES: 
Used RipX DAW PRO software (Mac OS X 14.4.1) to isolate vocals & instruments. 
No sounds have been added or removed from the original recording.

SOLO SET: Raised volume of vocals & lowered volume on guitar. Small eq adjustments to mid range & high range on vocals & low, mid & high range on guitar.

ELECTRIC SET: Raised the volume on the vocals & small eq used on the mid range and highs on vocals. Raised the volume of the guitar & small eq to mid & high range on guitars. All other stems of the recording remain the same as the original. 

CONVERSATIONS: Raised Mid range and high range 5 db & then lowered volume on entire track.

Small volume adjustments applied as needed throughout recording. 

There is an edit at 3:54 on track 05 on the original recording that remains on this remix.
