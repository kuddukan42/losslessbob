Notes: I wasn“t able to get one of the 800 tickets for this special club-gig, but 
fortunately my friend Erik got hold of a ticket - and when asked he was more than 
happy to try recording the show with my equipment. After nearly being caught at the 
entrance (he was saved by a couple of sandwiches in his backpack....) he did indeed 
succeed in getting a really nice recording of the complete show.

About the recording: Recorded 10 metres from the stage, with the mics in a really cool cap. 
The recording-level was maybe a tiny bit too high. On some of the songs outros, the levels 
get somewhat hot - the kick-drum being "the problem" - but I can“t hear any severe distortions. 
Also, with the size of the venue and the rather enthustiastic crowd ;-), you“ll of course 
have some audience-noise. I don“t think it“s that distracting, instead it gives the 
recording the feeling of "being there".

Hope you all will enjoy!!

And finally, a million thanks Erik for your hard work...the sandwiches must have tasted like heaven.....
