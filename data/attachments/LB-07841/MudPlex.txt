Review: 
This is a nice package that features three discs containing two complete, consecutive shows. The CDs are housed within a full color folding cardboard package with track list and venue info printed on the back. The goofy title comes from combining the names of the two venues included. Mud Island is an incredible stereo audience recording with a great mix and powerful in-your-face vocals. The quality of the Starplex is not nearly as good, and brings down the overall quality of the set. The performances are powerful and tight, and a good example of the surprise material updates for the Never Ending Tour. 
* Features Joe Walsh on guitar.
