(Caveat: I have used the label NET TAPER A to refer to a taper whose work does not
seem to circulate widely. All of the NET TAPER A recordings come from my source, who
received them directly from the taper. There is always the possibility of misidentification
since the taper may also have sent recordings which were not his own. My apologies if any
such misidentification has occurred in the present case.)

This recording seems to me to be different from the LB1992 recording. An example of crowd
noise difference is: At beginning of d1t5 on LB1992 there is, a few seconds before the
"one-two-three" countdown to the beginning of the song, a "wow" followed by a "thank you,
bobby". These shouts are missing on the present version. The marked volume fluctuation
noted for d1t1 on LB1992 is missing on the present version, though there is a certain 
amount of fluctuation.
