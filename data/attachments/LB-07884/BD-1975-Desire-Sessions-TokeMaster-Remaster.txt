Details on the Remaster:
Using PinkRobert's Desire Sessions CDs.
Matering done with NERO 8:
1. Volume reduced by 2-3 db, 
2. Left channel mixed 20% into right, Right mixed 20% into left.
3. Pitch was reduced by on all tracks except tracks 7 & 15 pitch was increased .
4. Vocals centered with balance.
5. All adjustments done "by ear".
