Bob Dylan, The Weberman phone calls, 1CDR

51min, Four tracks, one day, downloaded from a.b.m.s 7/02;  This fan release contains the Dylan-Weberman telephone conversations recorded by the self proclaimed 'Dylanologist' and long time Dylan antagonist A.J. Weberman on January 6, and  January 9, 1971. The telephone conversations were soon after released to the public as a Broadside/Folkways LP entitled "Bob Dylan vs. A.J. Weberman." The LP was soon pulled from the shelves by the legal clout of CBS. It has since become a high dollar collectible. This particular recording is taken from the super clean LP bootleg 'Grudge Match', which was directly recorded from a pristine 'official'  LP.
