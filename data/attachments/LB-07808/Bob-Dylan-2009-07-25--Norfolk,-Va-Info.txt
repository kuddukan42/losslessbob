The sound at Harbor Park was pretty boomy to my ears, even for a ball park.
The crowd was pretty enthusiastic even after being in the 90+F heat for a few hours. 
We did have a nice breeze from over the Elizabeth River to help keep things from being too oppresive.
There is some wind noise, as I forgot my screen, but it's not bad.


There was a group of women there on a Girl's Night Out thing I guess. One of them saw me recording and asked if it was video or audio. 
Thing is, she kept asking even after I told her. She didn't look drunk, and I only saw her drink water, but who knows.
Just before High Water, I checked the time left on the recorder, and she started to speak right over the mic. 
So I suggested that she say "Hi" to the folks that will download this recording.

She didn't say anything for the rest of the set. I hope I didn't wreck her good time.

So this recording is dedicated to her, whatever her name is.
