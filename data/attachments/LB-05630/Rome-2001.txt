Review:
This is part one of the two part interview from Rome on July 21, 2001. Dylan reveals more of himself than ever. The original tape has been cleaned up, and digitally enhanced so that the reporter's questions can now be heard. Dylan is so crystal clear that, at times, he seems to be sitting in the room with you. The package is simple, but elegant. The interview has been released on two separate CDs. The second half of the interview concludes on "Part 2

CD 2
Tracks:
Dylan talks on over 50 subjects, giving them about 1 minute each. The subjects are listed on the back of the CD insert.

Review:
This is the conclusion of the Rome interview from the first disc. As with the original release of the first part of the interview, Dylan's voice is crisp and clear. It seems as if he's in the room with you. The original tape has been cleaned up, and digitally enhanced so that the reporter's questions can now be heard. The package images, aesthetics, and inside insert are identical to the first disc. The only changes are the disc number and the content information. These two discs comprise a relatively rare set, released in a quantity of only 250.
