Bob Dylan & The Band
Ann Arbor, MI
February 2, 1974
AUD

Very good audience recording from the 74 tour.  I got this in a trade, so I am not sure of the previous lineage, but I used EAC to rip from the CD-Rs, and then FLAC Frontend to encode.  The artwork I got with the trade is not the artwork I am including, but the included artwork does match the show on the disks.

Great show.  This was the first time I saw Dylan or The Band, and it was a memorable experience.  Before the start of Like A Rolling Stone, the ushers (and much of the audience) swept down onto the main floor while the house lights were being turned on one section at a time.  I honestly thought the roof of the building was going to blow off from the whoosh of energy.  The music still gives me chills.

Enjoy & share!
