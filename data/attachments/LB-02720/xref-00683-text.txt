1979 THE FIRST GOSPEL TOUR
Introduction

The Gospel Tour that started in 1979 and continued in the spring of 1980, featured only gospel songs,
either performed by the backup singers, or by Bob Dylan backed by the band. No songs from his other
records were played. The setlist was almost constant from night to night. The audience responses were
confused, angry and sometimes outright hostile. Many came to see a rock and roll show and were
given a gospel concert. Dylan responded with long sermons between songs about the end of the world,
the battle of Armageddon and the second coming. These lengthy raps are included in the session
pages.

The show
A gospel concert consisted of:

Set 1 6 songs by the backing singers plus Terry Young on piano
8 or 9 songs by Bob Dylan and the band
1 song by one of the backing singers and the band
Set 2 7 songs by Bob Dylan and the band
Encore 2 songs by Bob Dylan and the band
