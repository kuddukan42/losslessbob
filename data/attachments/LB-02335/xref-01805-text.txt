RTM21 from The Box Set: Righteous To Me: 1993 Preboards

Contrast clause: This torrent differs from audience recording: Bob Dylan 1993-04-21 Monroe, Louisiana AUD. This torrent is a Pre-Board from the RTM box set, that means it is a line recording, probably hooked to a sound monitor, but it is not a soundboard (SBD) recording in the sense that it does not represent the feed going out to the audience. It frequently emphasizes several instruments and/or vocals at the expense of others. Most of the RTM box set recordings have Dylan's vocals up front, but often they are recorded too hot and have volume fluctuations as the taper tries to compensate. Some of them, however, are excellent recordings.

Lineage: Unknown.

Sound is mostly very good to excellent.  Some distortion in the first 3 songs due to high recording volume.
