This source has not been seen here since forever.  Unfortunately, it was filled with SBEs at every turn, so I fixed them and then re-encoded to SHN.

This is not LB-7649, which looks like a fixed version of LB-1036 too.  It was re-encoded to FLAC but not before "levels have been raised on this so it has a lot of bad clipping" (on some or all of it)(http://jokerman.org.uk/lb/detail/LB-07649.html).   

We received in '04 several DVDs of data files from someone in Australia for a slew of '92 Australia shows, but we had mislaid one of the DVDs.  I found it during our off day from work Monday (really bad snow storm clobbering the Midwest), and this Adelaide show is the first one I listened to, then decided to upload, and then realized I needed to fix.  I then looked back and saw I already posted everything from another DVD (1992-04-11 and later).  The shows in the current data DVD are dated March 18 - 29.  Like the other data DVD, I may post them all (after I fix them, which seems abundantly likely).
