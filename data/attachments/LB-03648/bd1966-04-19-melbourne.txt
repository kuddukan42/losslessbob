A Watchtower Remaster

Bob Dylan 
19 April 1966*
Festival Hall 
Melbourne, Victoria, Australia 
SBD recording

As with the April 13th show, the first song is clipped at the start.  Perhaps someone was a little slow turning the 
machine on.  This version contains fragment of Mr Tambourine Man which does not circulate widely and is not on any of 
the commercial boots for this date.
