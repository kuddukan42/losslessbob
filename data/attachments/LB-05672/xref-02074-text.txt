When I received this show in '06 [LB-04087] I spotted the microgaps and removed them track by track.  It was for my own personal use and I never circulated that version.  There was no particular pattern to the microgaps.  Some tracks had a microgap at the beginning, some at the end, others both, and a few had none.  There were microgaps of 0.001 second, and others as long as 0.02 second.  The microgaps fell on periods of crowd noise between tunes, so I doubt anyone, unless they were listening super attentively, would notice.  The removal process eliminated a total amount of 5 seconds of silence.  

So I suppose my reworking of LB-04087 is a parallel version of LB-05672, which was a fixed version of LB-04087 (microgaps removed with Wave Repair 4.9.2.).    

JTT put in fade ins and outs for three discs.  I didn't bother to alter that. I suppose if you burn CD 1 and CD 2, tracks 1-3 on one blank and everything that follows except CD 2, track 5 (Dylan talking) and CD 3, track 1 (band intros) on a second blank you can get everything on two discs.  

I renamed the tracks to a more typical style and created a .ffp file. 

Nothing has been commercially released.
