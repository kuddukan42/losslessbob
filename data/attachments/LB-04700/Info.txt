BOB DYLAN - Starlight Bowl, San Diego, CA.  September 6th 1989 "2nd Generation Transfer"

Good / Very Good Audience Recording. Setlist drawn completely from The 60s

Bob Dylan
Starlight Bowl
San Diego, CA
September 6th 1989

NOTE: I first circulated this recording on Dylantree around four to five years ago, that
transfer was from my Minidisc backup copy and included filler tracks. The San Diego tracks
only, were later remastered by Schubert from that same copy.

To my knowledge this is the first time a transfer direct from tape has circulated other than
among private collectors.

There are Low Level sounds at points during the recording, these are caused by low flying
planes. Also the sound drifts at times, I can only assume due to audience movement or breeze.
I have not attemted to recify these problems.

Soundforge 8 (Fades, Track Marks, Joins)>FLAC Level 6 Align on Sector Boundaries
