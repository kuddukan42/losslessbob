AUD(Sony D6>Realistic Omnidirectional in left channel)>Cass/0>Adobe Audition(Sony TC-KE500S>Edirol UA-1EX)>Flac(CDWav)

The master was recorded in only one channel so I used Audition to pan the mix into both channels
and remove a couple dropouts.  CDWav was used to cut the tracks and compress to FLAC.  
Track 1 has a few anomalies as I got the mic and levels set.  After a couple seconds, the levels 
and sound balance out pretty nicely.  Check the mp3 sample for quality.  
It's a decent AUD recording.  Sorry about the talking ; )
