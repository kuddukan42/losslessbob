Dylan at the Warfield
San Francisco Nov 11 1979
audience recording

An above average recording of an above average show from the legendary Warfield '79 residency..

This show was seeded before but this transfer was not. It's from a 2nd gen tape at best and the taper is unknown to me. Nearby talking makes it appear to be the same taper as LB 1887, without the tape damage (see http://jokerman.org.uk/lb/detail/LBM-year.html).

Seeded on Dime, March 2007. Please don't re-seed elsewhere while it's active here.
