I really enjoyed this show...Great set list... Bob seemed in a good mood & his voice sounded great.  Something funny happened
at the beginning of Standing in the Doorway, but Bob started the lyrics again & they pulled off an amazing version of 
this song...
Visions of Johanna , Girl of the North Country & Hollis Brown were the highlights for me - the band was tight & Dylan's
vocal phrasing kept everything interesting. This recording came out very good - a little audience noise - I was packed
in the GA crowd on the floor in front of the stage.
