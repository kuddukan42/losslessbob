BOB DYLAN - Empire Burlesque Originals & Outtakes (Transfer From 2nd Generation Cassettes)

These tapes were received around the time the album was released and a considerable time
before the first Vinyl Bootleg appeared. This is the first time I have transferred them
for burning to disc.

I have not compared quality with available sources but thought as circulating discs all
seem to be from commercial bootlegs this transfer may be of interest to collectors.
