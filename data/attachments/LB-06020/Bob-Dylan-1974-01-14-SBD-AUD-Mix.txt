Mix Notes

All songs are from the SBD source except the last eight tracks of disc 2 as they were not included on The Swingin' Pig's SBD release. The intros to I Shall Be Released and The Times They Are A-Changin' were patched with the AUD.

The AUD was speed corrected to correct the pitch (-.5 adjustment). No further processing was done to either recording.

All Mix speed correcting, patches and fades were done using Cakewalk Home Studio 2004.
