Recorded from the front row of the first baseline stands.  Bit distant, but I’m too old to fight the crowds in the heat.  The wind screws with the sound a bit.  Bass roll-off on to try to cut down on the echo coming from the home plate area.  Hopefully someone will have a more up front recording, especially to catch some of the dialog between Dylan and the band that was seeping out over the PA.  This show was as good as it can get with such a set up.  If Forgetful Heart was performed like this in front of a small venue, the audience would have exploded after it was over.  Positively brilliant, and lost on most people more than 100 feet away.  Tracks done on the fly so they are not perfect.  Enjoy
