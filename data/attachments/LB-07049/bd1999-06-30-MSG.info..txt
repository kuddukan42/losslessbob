Disc 2 had bonus tracks named after bd1999-06-25d2t06 and so on. They were actually from Eric Clapton & Friends concert at MSG to the Benefit Crossroads Centre in Antigua.

I just renamed all those tracks like that:

bd1999-06-25d2t06 -> bd1999-06-30d1t01
......

bd1999-06-25d2t14 -> bd1999-06-30d1t09

and put them apart on a third folder as bd1999-06-30d1.
