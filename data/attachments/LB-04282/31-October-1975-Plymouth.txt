Notes •
The circulating PA-tape also contains Oh Happy Day, sung by Joan Baez, Chestnut Mare with Roger McGuinn and the
Baez-McGuinn duet. Mary Hamilton and Love Song To A Stranger are not in circulation but are mentioned by Sloman.
• Mama, You Been On My Mind was performed only once during Tour 1974, in Philadelphia 6 January 1974.
• Sloman mentions the dedication David Crosby. His name can’t be heard on the tape.
• The reference to “Arthur” probably refers to the sound & guitar technician Art Rosato.
Official release
1 released in the movie RENALDO AND CLARA, 25 January 1978 (long version) and late 1978 (short version).
