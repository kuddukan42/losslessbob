I haven't seen this Dylan show torrented in the past year, so I thought I would put it up.  A very interesting show, with an interesting set list and a great rendition of Hard Rain.  This show was Dylan's Fairground show at the 2003 New Orleans Jazz and Heritage Festival.  He also did a show the next night at the Auditorium, and only repeated 4 songs on both nights.  (I will torrent that show next week)  One of the first shows with Freddy Koella on guitar, so there was some freshness (and sloppiness) in the arrangements.  Further reviews of the show at http://my.execpc.com/~billp61/042503r.html.

I got this in a trade, used EAC to extract it, and then edited the first track, which started with about 15 minutes of random crowd noise.  Once edited, used FLAC frontend to compress with sector boundary correction.
