Another superb show from this European leg.
 This is becoming a vintage period for Bobby
 Highlights for me,a coruscating "Highway 61"
 "John Brown" sends shivers through me and
 "Ain't Talkin"  full of menace.The band really is cooking.
 This is a nice crisp,clear capture,maybe not quite
 as good as some of the "establishment" recordings
 for which we are eternally grateful
 but highly listenable..
