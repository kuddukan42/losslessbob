Audience recording. Downloaded from a binary newsgroup (shn). SHN -> flac with shntool. Added metadata tags. 

One of my favorite unsung NET concerts (actually the 500th NET show, according to Olof!) from way back. Bob and the band in a jam-band mode, long songs and great lead guitar interplay between Bob, J.J. Jackson, and Cesar Diaz, who features on some of the songs. An exquisite audience recording, full of warmth, especially in the acoustic songs. Listen with good headphones and you'll be transported in time and place to a warm (I presume) Portugal night 18 years ago... The setlist is nothing unusual for the time, but the performances are outstanding: like I said, one of my favorite NET recordings. 

There are some minor digital defects that appear in the original SHNs I acquired from a binary newsgroup a decade or so ago. It's a sort of digital distortion that appears on some loud parts (especially at the beginning of Memphis Blues Again). There's no tape hiss, it's probably a DAT recording. 

This is my first upload, hope all goes well...
