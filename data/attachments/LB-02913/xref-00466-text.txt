Bob Dylan, 7/17/94, Krakow, Poland, Cracovia Football Stadium, Krakow, 1CDR

LB-2913, (64min), Schubert Additional Sound Master (Schubert ASM), surprisingly good sound. Got it as aud CD-R, short route to master.
