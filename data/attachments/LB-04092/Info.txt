BOB DYLAN - The Fox Warfield Theatre, San Francisco. November 15, 1980 (Full Show)

Excellent Audience Recording

Transfers from Disc and Tape and Artwork (included) by JTT (2006).


CDR>EAC>Soundforge 8>FLAC
Tape (Akai GX-95 Mk11>Soundforge 8>FLAC

I have compliled this recording from the two best sources I have available (see
details below).  Both recordings were very disjointed so I have attempted to
present it as a smooth running show for a more enjoyable experience.

Thanks to Les Kokay (discs recieved in trade) and the late John Green (tape recieved
many years ago).

Note: There is some debate as to whether "Love Minus Zero" is from another date,
it was incomplete on the Kokay discs. I havn't checked into it and as John passed
away can not check his original source. Either way it's a great show and fine quality.


Notes:
All Tracks Disc One & Track 13 Disc Two
taken from Les Kokay CDRs. 

Disc Two, Tracks 11,12,14 & 15 taken from tape recieved
from the late John Green's collection.

Sources were mono and the Right Channel was found to be
slightly cleaner than Left, therefore Right was fed to both Channels.
Left was not used.
