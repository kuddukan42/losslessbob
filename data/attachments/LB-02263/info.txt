Bob Dylan
Houston 25 Jan 1976

I believe this is an unusually spirited performance, although the recording is below average. This is actually a splice of two separate tapes. Both have been equalized. The show has been bootlegged several times.

Also performed but not on this recording is "Knocking on Heaven's Door" and "Ride 'Em Jewboy" by Kinky Friedman, with Dylan on guitar.

2.  When I Paint My Masterpiece
