Notes:
With God On Our Side is played only once during this tour, first time since London 9 May 1965, and contains the extra
lyrics
“I’ve learned to hate Russia, and China, and Korea,
and Vietnam, and Bulgaria, and Poland,
and South America, and Cuba, all of my whole life”
Live debut of The Water Is Wide.
