I figured why not blow the dust off another Mad Dog remaster of a performance from 1990. 
This is the second show after the infamous Toad's Place. Not the most perfect recording 
though I was able to lessen the hiss also on the louder parts there is some heavy bass 
saturation which I was able to make less intrusive. The performance is completely 
inspired by Bob and his band. I especially love his harp playing on this one.  Enjoy!!!
