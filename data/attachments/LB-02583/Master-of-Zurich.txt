Review from bobsboots.com:
Bob and the band are in perfect form at this venue. Everything that was wonderful about this tour seem to converge together for this incredible concert. The sound quality is fantastic. Instruments are mixed to perfection, and vocals are up front and in your face. What better way to end a magical night then to bring out some special guests. Bruce Springsteen's guitarists Nils Lofgren and Little Steven  jam with the band for an encore that brings down the house. 

From monicasdude:

DEEP: Crunchy, upfront amd outstanding sound quality on the Zurich show. Too tiresome to conjure up any more adjectives to describe Bob's performance, or the performance of the crack band during the Euro '98 trek, where Bob presented a revamped show that opened with 7 acoustic numbers. Suffice it to say that the Zurich show is strong and tight and finds Bob in top form. Quality falls a bit on the filler from Marseilles, despite the always welcome "Visions Of Johanna." 
rmd: A beautifully packaged, great sounding set. The vocals are clear and upfront and there is nice separation. A superb performance, brought to a rousing finish when Springsteen's guitarists Nils Lofgren and Steve Van Zandt join the band for a the final encore.
