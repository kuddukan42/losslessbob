I have tried my best to improve the original copy of this recording I had from an early trade in 2002 not so long after the show (thanks JRB), nice capture of a great concert but looking like gone through a heavy remaster. Most likely same recording and similar version as LB-3331.

Lineage: Cd-R (of unknown gen) > wav (eac secure mode) > wav (Adobe Audition 1.0 / asm) > flac (Flac Frontend 1.1.3 - align on SB option - level 8 - verify on) 

Sound process applied:

1. -3 db cut (deamplification)
2. simple bass lift (high end slightly modified at times)
3. gentle mid cut

Maybe also a very slight touch on a few spots to improve the resulting sound as possible when necessary. Tried to avoid harshiness and muddy bass just getting a thiner sound an not a real improvement so I had to look for a reasonable balance instead.
