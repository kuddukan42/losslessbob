BOB DYLAN - Stockholm, Sweden March 28th 2007 "Complete From Two Sources"

Bob Dylan
Globe Arena
Stockholm, Sweden
March 28th 2007 

This is a composite recording mastered from the following sources:

Source One - Torrent  #140615 (http://www.dimeadozen.org/torrents-details.php?id=140615)
Source Two - Torrent  #140618 (http://www.dimeadozen.org/torrents-details.php?id=140618)

Disc One, Tracks 01-09 and Disc Two, Tracks 01-06 (Source One)
Disc Two, Tracks 07-08 (Source Two)

Many thanks to the original tapers and hoping they don't mind their recordings being used.

I put this together until such times (hopefully) a complete recording will emerge.

The sound on 'Source One' was very good but one-sided (mostly right) and occasionally
drifted, I found this quite distracting, so I have converted to mono. The two tracks from
'Source Two' have also been converted to mono and slight treble lift applied to make a
better match.
A few digipops and stray claps have been removed and clapping on 'Nettie Moore'
reduced slightly.

Source: Downloads (see above)>FLAC to Wave>Soundforge 8 (Stereo to Mono, Level Correction,
Track Marks, Fades, EQ (Treble Lift)>FLAC Level 6 Align on Sector Boundaries.

Mastering & Artwork by JTT, April 2007
