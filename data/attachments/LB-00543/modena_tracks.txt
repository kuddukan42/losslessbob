
4.  TEMPLES IN FLAMES

  4.1 Introduction
 ~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
  The tour in Europe in the fall with Tom Petty & The Heartbreaker was
  very, very different from their previous tour in the US, True Confessions.
  Dylan again was the elderly bard. Stage lights were so turned down you
  could hardly see him. His singing was at times extremely erratic, both in
  terms of phrasing and lyrics. The sets were apparently completely random,
  as can be seen from the song charts below. The Dylan part of the show was
  also very short. All this: short set, dark stage and Dylan not saying a word,
  compared of course very unfavorably to the previous shows in Europe in the
  summer of '84 and a lot of people went home utterly disappointed.
  It must be said though that The Heartbreakers provided excellent backing
  throughout the tour and that some of the shows were really very good.


Recommended shows
  ~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
  Modena, Italy, September 12,
  Torino, Italy, September 13,
  Stockholm, September 26,
  London, October 17.
