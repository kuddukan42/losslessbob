\cf0 I gave this recording a name:  "First Sign of the Cross."  The reason for the name was that, after reading Heylin, I learned that the photos I have of Bob from that night wearing a shiny silver cross around his neck may have a certain significance.  Supposedly, at a show in San Diego someone threw a silver cross onto the stage, which Bob picked up and tucked into his pocket.  A week later, Bob appears onstage wearing a metal cross around his neck. Well, that was the Fort Worth show, and I was a 17 year old kid taking pictures.\
\
I traded/gave this recording out to only 3-4 people, before now.  The surfacing of T\'d5s [different] recording of this show at The Trader\'d5s Den prompted me offer this one up.  Enjoy.\
