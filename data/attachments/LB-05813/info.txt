Bob Dylan
San Antonio, Texas 11 May 1976
Mono audience recording.

A great show from Jeff Friedman's Golden Archives, but a below-average recording from a dinky seat. This has never been seeded like this- without some awful intermingling of the partial soundboard recording, and with EQ to compensate for the terrible mic. Please tell me how you like it.

The taper missed the first few tunes and overused the pause button and missed the opening and closing notes of most tunes. Still, I found it hard to stop listening to this show.
