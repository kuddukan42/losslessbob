	This dvd disk is a set of recordings from Spring 1996 taped
by an individual whom I will simply refer to as NET Taper A, since 
it appears to me that he desires anonymity (at least with respect to
his taping activities). All of these recordings were sent by the taper 
to my source, so they certainly count as"lowgen" recordings. Based on 
ancedotal evidence, it seems unlikely that these recordings have 
circulated in any extensive way, so they should be new to most 
collectors. The sound quality is, I believe,excellent.

The shows included on this disk:
1996-05-08
1996-05-12
1996-05-14
1996-05-16
1996-05-17
1996-05-18
