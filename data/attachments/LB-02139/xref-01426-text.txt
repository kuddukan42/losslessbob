This is not the Cedar 4 Remaster but in fact a remaster I did of Cedar 2 and though many say 
the remaster of Cedar 4 is the one to get I swear this is by far superior. I just compared
them again and yes this one has a warmer fuller sound plus the crowd on this recording
is less intrusive. I know many will dispute me still I just wanted to share this because 
before those three fateful London's there was this Paris which is quite possibly the show of 
this entire beatific tour! Ya I know he did Jokerman and Romance In Durango and so many other 
rarely played songs at the three legendary London shows and all I am saying is before that he 
did this exquisite and majestic performance in the City of Light!!! The whole thing astounds me 
but Senor (listen to that harp toward the end) and Visions well it doesn't get much better 
than this. ENJOY! ---  Charlespoet
