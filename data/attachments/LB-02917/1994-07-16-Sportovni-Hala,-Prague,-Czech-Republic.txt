BOB DYLAN

Sportovni Hala
Prague, Czech Republic
July 16, 1994

A gesture to kan144 who asked for this recording; and following his link gave a definite hint it should be upped; another superb recording: 

Sound: 9 (the taper starts messing with the levels during Jokerman, damn near scared me into cardiac arrest, Christ! But I'm glad he did as this is one sweet recording job. Levels are great, vocals are crystal clear, almost a little too upfront though as evidenced by the speaker shake when Bob gets hold of a long high note, heh heh, if they were all like this I'd be the happiest kid on the block. This gets a 9 based almost entirely on the big loud vocals, and why not?)

Highlights: Lay Lady Lay, I Shall Be Released, Blowin' in the Wind

Comments: Hey, you're having a Good Harp Day, take advantage, Bob!     
Bottom Line: Brilliant, please go trade this and then give copies to everybody you know to help circulate it. Traders need to realize that shows like this one can be sublime and transcend their pedestrian setlists. A top 5 show of the year candidate.
