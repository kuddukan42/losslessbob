Review (lifted off Bobsboots and included):
Crystal Cat created a wonderful series of issues known as the 2000 series to commemorate tour Y2K. The audience recordings are as good as it gets. The package aesthetics are very nice. All contain a 6 page full color booklet. All CDs have the same generic silk screen print, but with wide variations of color backgrounds. The cover features a ticket for the event. This series of 15 double CDs is extremely limited, with only 500 each glass mastered copies.
