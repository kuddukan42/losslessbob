This is not a full spectrum recording;  disc 1 appears to have little spectral density above 16kHz but disc 2, while not perfect, is much better.  Disc 1 had micro-gaps each side of the track break which I removed, disc 2 had only one such problem (d2t01-t02). Excellent sound, [A-].
