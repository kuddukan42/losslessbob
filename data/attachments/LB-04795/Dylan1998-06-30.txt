Notes.

 

This was a double bill with Van Morrison.

 

After Mama, You Been On My Mind Bob had a short discussion with Tony who told Larry to come over. A few smiles later Bob stepped up to the microphone and started Happy Birthday, Tony and Larry following close behind. I don't think that he actually got neither the chords nor the melody right, but it's the thought that counts, right? It was sung for a mysterious Suzie, that's all I know. After he had finished the song, Bob laughed and said: "We did play it in a different key at the end, didn't we?" and then he played it again! (Thanks to Carsten Wohlfeld).
