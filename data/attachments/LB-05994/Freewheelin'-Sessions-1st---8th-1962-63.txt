Lineage: impossible to give. This is an ongoing project, with songs of lesser audio-quality replaced over the years.
Thanks to my (then) brother in law who borrowed me the $ 18 for buying Great White Wonder.
What happened afterwards, I could hardly keep track…….
For reasons of pleasure of listening to them I shuffled them around; so there isn’t any sequence as given by Krogsgaard or Bjorner.
Programs used, Nero 5-8, Steinberg (wavelab 5).

Upload HC-staff permission granted.

This was exactly the same upload as on mininova from January, but without disc 4, called replacements.  There is a recent Hollow Horn Upload here, “let my poor voice be heard” vol. 1.  
I haven’t made a quality comparisons yet;  some songs could be a lot better on that compilation.

For purists and other people who make false claims:
These songs were played by Robert Allen Zimmerman, HE owns them. We are just humble listeners and caretakers with no status whatsoever attached !
So they were not played by Howlin’ Husky, who’s not even in the background howling, but who took the liberty making this compilation in August 2007.
