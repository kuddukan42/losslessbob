Bob Dylan
Yogi Berra Stadium
Montclair State University
Montclair, New Jersey
24 June 2005
 
source: Sony ECM-DS70P>Sony MZ-N10 Net MD
Location: ga on field
taper: BSORocks
 
This show was just amazing.  I went really to hear Willie Nelson, but I was absolutely floored by Dylan's exceptional performance. 
A first source was posted here shortly after the show...this one was taped from the ga field about 20 feet from the stage. I think it's a great recording and was pleasantly surprised with the results. 
Enjoy, and please let me know what you think. 

And as always, this is for personal use only. No selling of any kind.
