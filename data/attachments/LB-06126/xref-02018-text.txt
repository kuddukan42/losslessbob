=================

Many thanks to Bach and Vito who travelled to Australia in 2001 and recorded Bob's tour here.
They made a great collection of tapes and allowed all Bob fans to hear these fantastic shows.
2001 is my favourite Australian Bob tour of the NET era.
He played 66 different songs over the 9 shows and the performances were solid throughout.
I was lucky to see the Brisbane and Ballina shows and then get to listen to Bach and Vito's tapes of those and the other shows.
I gave Bach's excellent recording an eq mix for the 20th anniversary of this Brisbane show.
See if you like it.
All thanks to Bach and Vito.
The Brisbane Entertainment Centre is notorious for it's poor sound, however, they did a great job.
Bob and his band were pretty good too.
