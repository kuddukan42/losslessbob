I don't like compilation/matrixes and this is another one I think sounds terrible when played straight through. This combines a recording of the Dylan parts of the show with an inferior recording of the Band sets from an alternate taper. I EQ'd both recordings individually, with no attempt to match the sound between them. You'll probably want to use your tone controls.

Band parts are from a current torrent on HungerCity - unknown lineage.
Dylan parts are from Frank B.

Seeded on Dime July 2007. Please do not reseed elsewhere while it's active here.
