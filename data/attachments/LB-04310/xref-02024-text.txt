audiowhore repair project - 1976

source used: LB-4310 (master > CDR > EAC > FLAC)


============

Notes about this recording:
- all thanks to the taper for being there
- mono audience recording
- tape damage on tracks 11, 12, 13, 21
- pitch and speed were well off for the whole recording, the sound was too low and slow.
- NOTHING from this concert has been officially released.

Does any version circulate without the tape damage?
Please contact me if you have a better source.


What i did to LB-4310:
- pitch correct by 0.45 Semitones and speed correct by 97.43%
- fix SBEs
- eq mixing to better balance sound
- some hard limit on loud clapping
- tape flips, minor dropouts, microphine bumps cleaned/manually removed.
- volume adjustments
- NO noise or hiss reduction used.

Notes about other sources of this show:
- There is only one known circulating recording of this show.
LB-2934, LB-4310 and LB-9401 are all from the same source.
LB-9401 was in worse condition with 2 songs missing.
