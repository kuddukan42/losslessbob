*****************NOTE***********************************************************
This is an edited version of this show. I have only been able to find two 
circulating versions of this show. Both were incomplete. One version was 
missing all of the first song, and the second version was missing part of 
Master's of War. The version missing Master's is the more 'common' of the two. 
It is also the better quality of the two. What appears here is an edited version. 
I took the brief missing piece from the poorer source, and edited it into the 
better source, resulting in a complete show. Nothing was removed from the 
better source, only the missing part was filled in. I apologize if this 
edit offends you in some way. The edit begins right at the end of Drifter's
and is cross faded out following the 'eyes' lyric in Master's. It's
there if you listen, as you can hear it get a little more distant, but
otherwise it's a nice edit and the causal listen won't hear it. 
Unfortunately, I have no information on either of these sources in terms 
of mic's, recorder, etc.
