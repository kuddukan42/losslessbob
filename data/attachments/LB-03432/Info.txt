Bob Dylan at Philadelphia Town Hall
Autumn 1964
Flac files - 1:49 total

There is a remaster of this show currently on Dime. This is an alternate remaster.

Without doubt, this was an exceptional show.
The orginal recording is below average even for 1964, and has a nasty microphone resonance I've tried to mininmize. I used EQ but no hiss reduction. If you need clean recordings with no hiss, you should surely skip this.

This came from the taper many years ago without a performance date. People have offered various dates in September and November but no documentation has been cited.

Please do not reseed on another tracker.
