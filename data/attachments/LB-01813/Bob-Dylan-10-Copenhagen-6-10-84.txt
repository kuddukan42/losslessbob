This is the 10th Show of Bob Dylan's 1984 tour with former Rolling Stones guitarist 
Mick Taylor.  After the unremarkable Gothenburg show the band comes back to life here 
at Copenhagen.  It started raining during show before "Maggie's Farm" (you can hear the 
rain drops, that is not dig noise) and Dylan request an umbrella from the crowd at one 
point.  During the first half of the show, Dylan and the band seem to try to make up for 
the rain by giving the crowd a great show.  They are halfway successful but by the end of 
the show even the band were showing signs of being miserable.  Dylan it seems toys with 
the idea of giving the crowd a special treat as he begins strumming the opening intro to 
"Lay Lady Lay" but instead he goes into  "Its All Over Now Baby Blue."  As usual 
Santana ruins a few good songs with his horrible tone and solos that seemingly don't fit 
in the song.  Luckily, he only played on the last few songs.

Right now is the 20th Anniversary of those shows and I thought it would be cool to 
release each show to STG on the date of the actual show plus 20 years.  So, here is the 
tenth show; only 17 more!!!  We'll see if I can keep up with the rest of the tour.
