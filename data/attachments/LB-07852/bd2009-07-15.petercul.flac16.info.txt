These recordings are done pretty econo... recorded straight to Edirol R-09 with built in mics. The copied from SD card to my Mac and cunt into tracks exported to FLAC with  Amadeus Pro. These will convert back to 24/48 audio. \

These recordings are not the best and not the worst. There is unfortunately a good deal of talking if you are easily distracted. I find this to be a very unfortunately part of modern concert going, even the seeming "biggest fan" talks the whole show through. Other then that sound quality is good with the Johnny Cougar show a bit boomy in the heavier parts.
