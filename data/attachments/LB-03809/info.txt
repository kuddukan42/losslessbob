Bob Dylan
Hartford Connecticut Civic Center
24 Nov 1975

A complete show from this legendary tour.

This recording has been seeded before but this is a new transfer, made from the original stereo cassettes taped and generously lent by Marty K, who wants it to be widely available.

Thanks, Marty!

There are some low-level clicks which are on the original recording. This show was also taped by Simon M. who (probably) captured only the Dylan sets.

Seeded on Dime June 2006. Please do not reseed elsewhere without this info file and any relevant comments.
