This is for Robert Onthis who has given us so
many great Dylan shows. I taped it at Tilles
Center, C.W. Post college, October 11, 1990.

Sony D5 cassette recorder, my absolute favorite
machine of all times. I use a DAT now on those
rare occassions when I actually go to a show but I'm
really not sure that it's any better than the
D5. Mikes were probably the single point stereo,
maybe the Sony 939. Something like that.

I think there is a short cut in Rolling Stone when
the machine got switched off for a second. I walked
out thinking that the show was very short and it was,
but I liked it a lot. I don't think I missed anything,
I think it was just short. A one disc wonder.

There was also a lot of bass which doesn't seem to have
made the tape.

No songlist, no cover art. I do have a ticket stub so
if anyone cares I'll scan it and post it.
