2001-03-03 Tokyo, Japan Setlist and Information 

Show Information 
This 2 CD set, graciously sent by Paulie Best (who received it graciously from Dan Marsh), is a very good audience recording, of a great Dylan 2001 show.
The setlist is solid and Dylan is in fine form (occasionally missing a line or two, of course).
The only problem I can find is a second long glitch in the middle of track 3, Desolation Row.
Other than that, WOW! 

Extra Information
Thanks to Tobias, we now have artwork for this show.

Disc Lengths: This set is on 2 CDs of length 74min (650mb)
