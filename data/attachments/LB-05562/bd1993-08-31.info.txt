Bob Dylan
Pine Knob Music Theatre
Clarkston, Michigan
August 31, 1993

I made a request suggested by a couple of friends who wanted this. Someone came along to help, but for some reason his/her upload fulfilling my request did never work. I was talking about this with my friend Marcos, the one who asked me first, when someone else heard my words and declared, "I got that show!". Very generous he inmediately offered to pass it through for both of us (Un millon de gracias, Valentin!!!). So here it is, for everybody else!
Sound quality is very good, I'd say excellent indeed. Some chat not so intrusive.

Disc #2, as I received it, included several bonus tracks, all 1993 performances. I thought it would be right to preserve them and be respectful with the original, except for the last 3 tracks (lower quality recordings) that I found of less interest, so I'm also including just those few additional files from 1993 USA fall tour that I felt appropriate to fit in this set.

My copy had just a few flaws I managed to repair with Adobe Audition:

d1t09 scratches from 7:36.825 to 7:37.050 (fixed applying slight click-pop elimination effect)
d2t02 several glitches from 0.36.302 to 0.36.325 (fixed by small patches of clean parts from the opposite channel, a few samples had to be removed to make it perfectly seamless)

Added also 1.330 sec. silence to the last track of Clarkston show, d2t03.

I did not harm the sound in any way, but for described work on the mentioned problems.

Fingerprint:  d1t08 @ 0:06 a woman asks the man she's chatting with (probably the taper himself), "it's wise...ohhh... what did you tape before?" or something like that, I'm not certain about what I understood, but the fingerprint is clear anyway.

Lineage: Cd-R (of unknown gen) > wav (EAC secure mode) > Adobe Audition 1.0 (clean up) > flac (Flac Frontend - align on SB option - level 8 -  verify on)
