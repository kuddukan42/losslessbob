Review: The Great Mr. Craig Pinkerton @ Bob's Boots

A great quality audience recording of a fun filled, good time show. The vocals are up front. The music is well mixed. A touch murkier sounding than some of the other circulating tapes of this tour ... but just a touch. Don't let it keep you away from this gem. The title track is magical. The bonus tracks are well chosen. The CDs are silk screened in black with the concert poster of this show. The package aesthetics and images are nice. The only minor problem with the package is a poorly designed, internally hinged jewel box that can actually damage the U-card as it swings open

Review: The Great Monica's Dude (possibly an alias) 

http://www.angelfire.com/wa/monicasdude/

DEEP: A delightful read of the spring 2000 Berlin show that does not disappoint. A vibrant, upbeat performance of a great setlist in upfront, full-bodied sound with lots of volume. Five bonus tracks are well chosen (but in lesser quality).
