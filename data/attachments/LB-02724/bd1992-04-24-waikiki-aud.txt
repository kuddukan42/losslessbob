Bob Dylan
April 24 1992 
Waikiki Shell  
Waikiki, Hawaii 

I received this audience recorded show in the form of audio disks from
a collector who characterizes most of his recordings as lowgen, but 
I did not receive any specific information concerning this item (hence
the lowgen? designation below):

Taper: unidentified.
Lineage: lowgen (?) audio disks > (Feurio) wav > (flacfrontend) > flac.

The crowd was a boisterous one and those with a low tolerance for being 
reminded what it is like to hear Dylan live perhaps should pass on this.
On the other hand, for the most part, the acoustic numbers like Golden
Vanity, Little Moses, Love Minus Zero/ No Limit, etc., are not disrupted.
Blowin' in the Wind is an exception. (There is a very definite 
fingerprint to this recording: right before the intro, someone asks
"you don't have matches?" followed by a laugh...)
