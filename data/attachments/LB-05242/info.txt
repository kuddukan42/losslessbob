Bob Dylan
Los Angeles Inglewood Forum
13 Feb 1974

This may be my favorite show from this leg of the '74 tour. The seat is good, but the recording is below average and noisy and pumped, with a bit of dreaded clap-along. Of those who actually listen to their torrents, few will enjoy it.

My thanks to the taper, JF (who sits next to my all-time favorite squealer).
