Review: 
Two days in November 1994 found Dylan and the band in New York City in rare form. 
The pressure and excitement was on as the taping began for what would be yet another milestone for the maestro. 
This two CD set captures the raw, unedited shows right off of the mix board. 
The sound quality aproaches that of the official release. 
AFTER you purchase the official issue, indulge in this wonderful, complete compilation and hear the shows as they  were played. 
This is not a substitute for the official release. It's a companion volume.
