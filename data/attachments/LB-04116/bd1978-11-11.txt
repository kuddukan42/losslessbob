In honor of Bob's show tonight in Vancouver, here is my first Dylan show, which took place 27 years and eleven months ago tonight in the very same venue.

This recording is quite dynamic -- loud parts are pretty loud, quiet parts are very quiet.  The hiss is quite audible during quiet passages.  I don't know if recording levels were low on the master, and an attempt to compensate was made at some point during the transfers, or what.  In any case, some parts of it sound not too bad.  There are some flaws, for sure.  The first track fades in.  Other fades were on my source tape.  Volume seems to fluctuate a bit here and there.  In spite of the flaws, I'm seeding this in the hope that somebody out there can put up a better one.  This show does circulate, and I have no idea how this compares with any other version that may be out there.  I've been unable to get this in a trade, and I got tired of waiting for it to show up.  On the positive side, it's relatively low gen and the lineage is known.
