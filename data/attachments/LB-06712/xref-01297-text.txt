Bob Dylan
Blood on the Tracks
Missing tracks from the test-pressing Album
Sept-dec 1974

These are, the never released tracks of the Test-Pressing of Blood on the Tracks end of 1974.
Ripped from the then made withdrawn LP.

01. tangled up in blue
04. Idiot wind
07. Lily, Rosemary and the Jack of Hearts
08. If you see her, say hello




Complete Test-Pressing :
01. Tangled up in blue : not the bootleg series 1-3 version
02. simple twist of fate: as the official released album
03. You're a big girl now: Biograph version
04. Idiot wind : not the bootleg series 1-3 version. Take note of the organ !
05. You're gonna make me lonesome when you go: as later released

06. Meet me in the morning: as later released
07. Lilly, Rosemary and the Jack of hearts: acoustic & never released
08. If you see her, say hello: a different version than on the bootleg series 1-3
09. Shelter from the storm : as later released
10. Buckets of rain : as later released
