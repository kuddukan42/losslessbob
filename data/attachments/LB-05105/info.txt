Bb Dylan - 1978_0607 Los Angeles, California, Universal Amphitheatre 2CD


I received this recording back in the 80“s from a Scandinavian trader who got it from the taper. The collector was kind enough to send me his tapes to my place, so that I could copy it with a Nakamichi Dragon and an a CR7 using high quality cassettes.
I put the whole thing on CD some years back without using any noise reduction or EQ.

I think it“s one of the very best 78 recordings (in terms of soundquality); maybe even better than some of the current digital recordings. And, of course, it“s one of the best shows from this great tour. I hope it will be an upgrade for some collectors.

From the same source exists a recording of LA 1978-06-03 which misses the first 5 tracks.
