Never since taking up collecting Dylan seriously in 1995 have I seen this 89min tape on any list, so I take the liberty to claim this is an uncirculated tape in this form (previously rumoured to exist). Unlike any other "rumoured" 22nd Jan 1980 tapes, this one is genuine. You can tell that by either comparing it with a widely circulating 33min excerpt of this tape (songs 1-6) or 38min soundboard tape (songs 4-7 & 9-12; juxtaposed with the present recording in this torrent for all non-believers).
