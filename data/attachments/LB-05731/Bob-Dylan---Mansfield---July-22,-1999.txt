Bob Dylan
July 22, 1999
Tweeter Center
Mansfield, MA  USA
Recorded by Unwanted Man Music

AT853H -> D7 -> Tascam CD-RW750 -> CD Wave -> FLAC
I adjusted the levels of some songs.

This was my last Dylan show from 1999 not on CD.  My seat was on the right side, in the 7th row, so the sound could be better, but it’s still not bad.  The crowd around me was a little chatty at times, and there were some loud clappers, too.  The first few seconds of the first song are missing.
