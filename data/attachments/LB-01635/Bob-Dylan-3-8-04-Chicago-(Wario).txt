From Park West's Website:

As one of Chicago's best equipped multimedia facilities, this historic theatre has been an integral part of the entertainment scene since it opened in the 1920's. Twenty years later the building became the Town Theater, a neighborhood movie house, until 1970 when it was transformed into the Chicago landmark that it is today. Since then Park West has been the premier concert location for nationally recognized entertainers AND the favorite meeting place for top corporations. Combining maximum flexibility with superior quality, the professionals at Park West are ready to make your event a success. For everything from meetings and presentations to parties and fashion shows, Park West is the room of choice.
