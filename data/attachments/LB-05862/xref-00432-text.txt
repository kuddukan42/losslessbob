11/11/81 New Orleans 

This is a partial soundboard  of New Orleans 11 Nov 81.
The final track (1:30s It's All In The Game) is also available on the 12 Nov 81 PA tape. It is better quality on that PA tape (included in the Chopen set) (included as a bonus here as well). Additionally 'When You Gonna Wake Up' and 'In the Garden' are also confirmed as being from this (11th nov 81) show.
Unfortunately an mp3 source was used for the "You Can't Kill an Idea Pt 1 & 2" (12 Nov 81) bootleg.

This is #11 of my 1981 additions / upgrades.
