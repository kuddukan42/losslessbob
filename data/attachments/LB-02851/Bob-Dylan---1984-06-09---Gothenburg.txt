Bob Dylan
"Extra Dylan" bootleg
1984-06-09
Ullevi Stadion, Gothenburg, Sweden

Haven't seen this circulate much in the digital age--this is a new seed from a relatively unknown first-gen recording. An excellent stereo audience tape, made during the "Infidels" tour. Sounds like taper was near a speaker stack, with clear guitars, bass, drums, vocals, but dynamic range a bit limited. The original bootleg also included two tracks from the next night's concert in Copenhagen, included the only known performance by Dylan of Willie Nelson's "Why Do I Have To Choose?" Note the two acoustic "mini-sets."

Artwork included in torrent.
