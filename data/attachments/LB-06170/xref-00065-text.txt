Bob Dylan – Atlanta, 9 February 2002

Production notes

This is HOJO ALD/Audience mix done by Jambo with a small amount of assitance from hodgo5, it's a gift to the guys on 
Hunger city for all the wonderful uploads in answer to our requests.

This a mix of two sources, the soundboard/ALD recording, which first circulated on cd-r in late 2002 and was used on 
boots such as “All Ages Catch Dylan,” and an audience tape of the same show. Although the sound quality on the SBD 
source was excellent it suffered from having practically no audience sound at all, making the performance sound very 
sterile. Hopefully this mix adds a bit more life to the show.

The mix was done in Cubase, with a little more top end added to both sources and the remix then run through a limiter 
to boost levels. Otherwise both sources were left pretty much as they were apart from a slight edit before 
“All Along The Watchtower” to cut out over loud audience talking. Unfortunately there were a few clicks on the 
soundboard source used, again most noticeably at the start of “All Along The Watchtower," and these had to be left in.
