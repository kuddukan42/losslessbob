Source: Low-gen audio disks > (dBpowerAMP set to compression level 6) flac.

Note: We did not see a non-commercial boot source for this date at LB. The commercial boot for this date, Live in Montreux 1994, is
incomplete,missing the opening track "Jokerman". This low-gen recording does have the missing track, but it is incomplete and starts
off very disant and also fades out for a moment before the applause begins. However, a complete "Jokerman" from a different master
has been added on at the end of the recording. I have no specific information about this alternative complete track or the master from
which it derives. Given the incomplete nature of "Jokerman" on the Legendary Taper J recording of the show, it is quite possible that 
the commercial boot derives from this recording, but I have not had an opportunity to listen to the commercial boot.
