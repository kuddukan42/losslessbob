Bob Dylan
Tokyo 3Mar78

"liberated bootleg". Sound is B minus.

After the Tokyo debut, the gang went to Osaka where they improved the groove. Then back to Tokyo for 5 more in a bit more relaxed style. The 3Mar and 4Mar show are from RK Records.

Lineage: "liberated bootleg", equalized.
