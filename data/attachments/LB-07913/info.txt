Just for Fun!

It should have been in the bin, but it has survived.

This tape circulated many years ago. At the time there were no Santa Monica tapes in circulation and this one was claimed to be 18 Nov 1979. It was later discredited and became known as the 'fake tape'.

It is dreadful quality and funs fast. I think most of Dylan's spoken comments have been edited out so it makes identification difficult. I haven't identified the source (or sources), so you might like to have a go.

No prizes for working it out, but please let us know if you work it out!
