Front of board approximately 6 meters from PA-speakers on right hand  
side -> Core Sound Binaurals (on sunglasses in the curls) -> homemade 
battery box (flat) -> SONY D7 -> hard drive via pro sound card -> 
mastered in Cool Edit Pro (minor amplification and hard limiting). Some 
'howls' (see below) has been edited out.

You will hear a terrible howling monkey, who came and stood right 
behind me from "Black Coat" until I moved away from him. In between he 
tried to assist Bob on voice, and during "Tweedle" he reached the 
heights of what seemed to be a synthetic orgasm. After "Nowhere" I 
couldn't stand him any longer, and as I at the same time began to be 
afraid of getting drunk from breathing in what he was breathing out, I 
moved in safety.
