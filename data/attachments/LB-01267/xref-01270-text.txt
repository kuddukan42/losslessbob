be getting the rest of them and in the meantime perhaps some other beatific soul could post
the first four Hammersmith's) plus the complete '90 Paris Residency -- all of them Holy Grails!
I very much want to thank Chuck for sending me these explosive shows in the first place. 
His generosity, as so many of us already know, is truly inspiring (like Bob's music!!!). 
Anyone out there with a copy of the Bauldie article about these shows please email me a copy - 
THANK YOU!!!!!!!  charlespoet@adelphia.net
