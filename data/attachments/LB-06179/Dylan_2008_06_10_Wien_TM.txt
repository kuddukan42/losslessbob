by the way, I had three seats during the concert. 
The first (and normal) was on the right side from one of the last rows in the floor.
During the Introduction I went to free seat to row 22, seat 41 of the eastgallery (I think you can't hear it).
But there was a lot of trouble with people who have been to late and looked for their seat (I cannot understand these people).
So I had to give up my seat after 15 minutes again and changed to row 23, seat 41 (for the rest of the concert).
You can hear this last change on d1t3 (3:10-3:48).
