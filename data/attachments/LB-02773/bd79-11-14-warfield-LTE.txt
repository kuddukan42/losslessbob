Legendary Tapers Series #23
Bob Dylan
Fox Warfield Theatre, San Francisco

Offmaster of an audience recording taped by Legendary Taper E.

The recording, like the other LTE recordings in this series, lacks all eight songs 
performed by the band. More surpisingly, it also lacks "Precious Angel" (which was
performed after ""Man Gave Names to All the Animals"),as well as Dylan's rap preceding 
"Solid Rock." The absence of "Precious Angel" on this recording has led some researchers
to not include it in the setlist, but there is apparently an alternative recording of
the show which while including the band songs, is more severely incomplete with 
respect to Dylan's performance than the LTE recording, but does include "Precious
Angel." 

(Incidentally, if any one has this alternative recording, I would happily 
trade for a copy. According to the "Gospel Project", the performance of "Precious
Angel" is outstanding.)
