BOB DYLAN - Mid South Coliseum, Memphis, Tennessee.  December 1st 1978 "Transfer from 2nd Generation Tapes"

Good to Excellent Stereo Audience Recording (Possibly Uncirculted)

Bob Dylan
Mid South Coliseum
Memphis
Tennessee
December 1st 1978

I received this recording in 1979 with notes that it was a complete recording
compiled from two sources. I don't usually agree with mixed source shows but
recently listened to it and found that although very disjointed the bulk of it
is possibly one of the best 1978 recordings I have. Two tracks were very poor
and I have left them out (but included them seperately in the torrent for anyone
who may wish to insert them). All bad joins have been either faded or seemlessly
joined to give a more pleasurable listening experience, I hate nothing more than
poorly edited shows with abrupt track ends etc.
There are a few seconds missing at the starts of I think three tracks, but nothing
destructive or they would have been omitted. Playback levels between the two
sources have been adjusted as near as possible.

As with all my transfers NO Normalisation or other alterations have been made
to the sound unless stated.

Source: Master Tapes (Main Source most likely Uher Open Reel)>2 x Sony CHF D90 (GS)>
Sony D3 (Playback)>Soundforge 8 (Track Marks, Edits, Fades, Level Adjustment & Balance)>
FLAC (Level 6, Align on sector boundaries)
