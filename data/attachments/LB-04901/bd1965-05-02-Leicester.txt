*She Belongs to Me may not be from this date.  The source is from the DLB outtakes, who attribute it to Unknown Location April/May 1965.  Research by Bob Stacey and myself place it at either Leicester, Sheffield or maybe Birmingham, with Leicester (because of the similar camera angle to Hattie Carroll) taking prime position.
