Bob Dylan - 1992-04-13 Sydney (JLT - shn)

JLT/LK TAPES
COMPLETE SET
I'm sharing some concerts from the 1992 Australian Tour that I have received from a friend. He received them straight from Les Kokay, who has agreed to openly publish them. The only information that came to me was:

"These files are exactly as I received them, no changes made in any form.
Thanks to the taper, to LK for making them available, and to my friend Marcos for sharing them with me."

I've added the info file and a new .md5
Setlist below from the Olof Bjorner Page: http://bjorner.com/still.htm

State Theatre Sydney, New South Wales, Australia 
13 April 1992 

1. Don't Let Your Deal Go Down (trad.) 
2. Lay Lady Lay 
3. Everything Is Broken 
4. Just Like A Woman 
5. Tangled Up In Blue 
6. She Belongs To Me 
7. Maggie's Farm 
8. Love Minus Zero/No Limit 
9. Little Moses (Bert A. Williams/Earle C. Jones) 
10. Gates Of Eden 
11. Mr. Tambourine Man 
12. Cat's In The Well 
13. Idiot Wind 
14. The Times They Are A-Changin' 
15. Highway 61 Revisited 
16. Absolutely Sweet Marie 
17. All Along The Watchtower 
18. Blowin' In The Wind 

Concert # 380 of The Never-Ending Tour. Concert # 16 of the 1992 Tour Of Australia. 1992 concert # 16. Concert # 16 with the 6th Never-Ending Tour band: 
Bob Dylan (vocal & guitar), Bucky Baxter (pedal steel guitar & electric slide guitar), John Jackson (guitar), Tony Garnier (bass), Ian Wallace (drums). 
8, 9, 18 Bob Dylan (acoustic guitar & vocal). 
4, 5, 8, 9, 11, 14, 15 16, 18 Bob Dylan harmonica. 
10 acoustic with the band except drums. 
11, 14 acoustic with the band. 
5, 14 Bucky Baxter (mandolin). 
10, 11 Bucky Baxter (dobro or acoustic slide). 
15 Bucky Baxter (electric slide guitar). 

BobTalk: 
Thanks everybody! For all you curious people out there: that was called Lay Lady Lay! That was off one of my own records for those of you who don't know it. This is off my new, sort of my new record. Another one of my animal songs called Cat's In The Well. 

6 new songs (33%) compared to previous concert. 1 new song for this tour. 
Stereo audience recording, 95 minutes. Session info updated 28 March 1993.

Thanks to everybody involved!!
