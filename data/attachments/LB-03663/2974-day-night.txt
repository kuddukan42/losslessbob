A mixed review for this title. Issued in a mini-LP type jacket, the front is simple and elegant. 
The stark black and white Jan Persson photo from 1978 is strikingly juxtaposed with the neon glow tube title. 
The title derives from the fact of the set presenting both day and night shows. It's nice to have both performances in one set. On the other hand, the sound quality is a little disappointing. There are much better representations of the 1974 tour.
