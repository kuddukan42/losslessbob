The Rolling Thunder Revue
Niagara NY Convention Center, Nov 15 1975 late show. Audience recording.
Dylan sets only, plus 3 songs from the Baez set and one chestnut from McGuinn

This is a fine enjoyable show from this famous tour, in average recording quality.

Adapted from LK's collection, with lots of fixups (speed, eq, dehum, re-channel, re-cue, but no NR or dehiss.)  Apparently includes a previous MD generation.

There is an alternate taper's recording from this show, which is cleaner but has much echo.

Seeded on Dylantree Dec 2006. Please do not reseed elsewhere while it's active here.
