SOURCES:

16th Late  - CDR at time of First Circulation
17th Early - Torrent Download, June 2008 (Tracks 4 & 5 replaced from Remaster torrent
by "mixedup", August 7th 2008). Several other glitches removed from other tracks.
17th Late  - "Live at The Supper Club" (Commercial Bootleg *) Torrent Download

NOTE: * "Live at The Supper Club" is NOT sourced from my original "clean-up"

16th Early, 17th Early and 17th Late > Download > FLAC Frontend > Soundforge 9 > FLAC Frontend

levels of the four shows made consistant.

Thanks to those who made the original sources available and to the uploaders.
