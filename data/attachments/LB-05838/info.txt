Bob Dylan
Houston Nov 12 1981

Here is yet another surprise from Jeff Friedman's Golden Archives. It's been bootlegged on CD, but this copy sounds a bit better and there are some title differences. Thanks, Jeff !

(Neither of us actually think there's anything exceptional about the show but it generates lots of comments.)

This comes from the same ultimate source as the various partial, intermingled and resequenced New Orleans recordings from the 10th and 11th. Because of all this diddling, I'm confident that the French-language introduction on this is actually from the 10th, given that New Orleans has many French speakers; the separate audience tape of the 11th has a similar French intro; and really- speaking French to Texans is just wacky.

/Lowgen
