      Review: 
      This 3-CD  release opens with the Fairfax, Virginia, show of November 22, 
      2002; and goes on to include an entire disc of highlights from the fall 
      tour. The performances are on the money. Bob's piano playing is great, and 
      blends perfectly into the mix. The vocals are powerfully up front. The 
      many surprises include a delicate version of Zevon's Accidentally Like A 
      Martyr with fantastic harmony vocals; Brown Sugar that rocks as hard as 
      any Stones concert;  A magically reworked Shelter From The Storm; and so 
      much more! The sound quality is good and consistent throughout. The extra 
      bonus material is very well chosen.  The information here is as 
      transcribed from the package. The package aesthetics are quite nice, with 
      some great photos. It all comes in a double thick jewel case to 
      accommodate the set.
