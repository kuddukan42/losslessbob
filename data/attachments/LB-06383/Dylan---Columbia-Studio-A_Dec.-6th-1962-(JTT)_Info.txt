BOB DYLAN - Columbia Studio A, December 6th 1962 (Low Generation Tape Transfer)

When I first received "For Sale Or Just On The Shelf" as a vine on Dylantree I noticed that
the Dec. 06 1962 Session was running slow and also realised I had a Stereo copy. I re-edited
the complete disc and inserted my superior source.

Unfortunately the only way I could edit the disc was to copy to Minidisc and then to Pioneer
Standalone CD Recorder. I then sent the disc to a contact in the U.S. and he was so pleased with 
the result he Re-vined the disc *(see LB entry below).

I don't know which version of "For Sale Or Just On The Shelf" circulates now but if it is my
upgrade then Vol. 1, Disc 2 is from Minidisc source. I believe the Hollow Horn disc was a
clone of "For Sale Or Just On The Shelf" so that too could be from this source.

This Torrent represents the first time this Session has been Torrented (by me) direct from
Tape to Hard Drive fully lossless.
