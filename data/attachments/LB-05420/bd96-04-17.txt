Bob Dylan, 4/17/96, Patrick Gymnasium, University Of Vermont, Burlington, Vermont, 2CDR 

Taper: SG
Source: low-gen audio disks > (dBpowerAMP set to compression level 6) flac.

Note: There are channel problems on d2,but for the most part do not interefere with the songs themselves until
 d2 t7, where they are severe and render the song unlistenable. Checking the lists of a couple fine collectors, 
both note the channel problems in the SG recording. There is one entry for this date in the Losslessbob archive, LB-501. 
Since there is no mention of channel problems for LB-501, it would appear to be derived from a different master.
