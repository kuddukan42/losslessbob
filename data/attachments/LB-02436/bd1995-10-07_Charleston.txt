gopherstick's review:
October 7 Charleston 
Sound: 8.5 (the vocals are out in front along with the guitars, especially on the acoustic numbers, the separation is just fine so just sit back and enjoy) 
Highlights: A mind-blowing One Too Many Mornings
Comments: The crowd is having a good time, but thankfully not at our covert expense. 
Bottom Line: Average solid show until the end when Bob pulls out the the top shelf vocals for One Too Many Mornings and sends the Good Chills straight down your spine for a good few minutes. If you like the tune seek out the show, the rest is worth hearing as well.
