Craig's Review: www.bobsboots.com

This set contains the full show. Forever Young is not listed on the insert, but it is indeed on the second disc on track number five. This creates a total of thirteen tracks on disc two. It's a fairly good audience recording of a solid performance. Highlights include the TOOM material and an erie Gates Of Eden. The artwork for this set is not quite as nice. The front cover is a joke. When will manufacturers learn that the folks who shell out a premium for these titles want to see some quality photos and aesthetics? There's nothing wrong with including a caricature inside the package somewhere... but instead, the insert contains 15 mini photos (any one of which would have made a better cover).
