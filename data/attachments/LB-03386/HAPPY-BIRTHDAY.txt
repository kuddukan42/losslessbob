Review: 
This is a fairly nice two disc set from newcomer Million Miles. The audience recording is great quality, and the fact that the show is on Dylan's birthday makes it a little more unique than the standard fare... as well as a couple interesting song switches. Aesthetics are clean and simple. The insert back shows an entrance ticket to the show, and erroneously states  that the front cover picture had been taken at the same show. The audience recording is quite nice.
