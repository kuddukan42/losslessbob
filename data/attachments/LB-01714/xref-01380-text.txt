There is only one source:  LB-1714. jokerman.org calls this recording a C, which means it is for completists only, I suppose.  I find the sound distant but decent but the distractions (the drop outs, the girl talking) are annoying.   

In '10, Chopen posted the show but referenced no LB number.  Of course, it had to be sourced from LB-1714.  jokerman.org identified the upload as "a close EAC match with same digital flaws plus it had discontinuities between tracks" - which usually means there is an additional FLAC > WAV > FLAC generation.  His upload was 446.48 mb. So it technically wasn't LB-1714.   

But then, neither is mine.  I am posting a fixed LB-1714 which had the original matching fingerprint (see below).  The show has been fixed because there were SBEs on every track.   

But I didn't otherwise alter the audio.
