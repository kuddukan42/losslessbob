LB-2112 indicates a version complete with sets by backing singers. Unkown if this source is alternate to LB-2112 or same but incomplete copy.

Gospel Project http://www.angelfire.com/rant/gospelproject/ indicates one source recording (271179RCMD) with detailed notes for each song/flaws and list of missing songs that match this copy. The webpage identifies the venue as San Diego, CA on the main page but incorrectly as Tempe, AZ when following link to the 27th. In addition, the setlist on TOP of the webpage mistakenly includes "Put you Hand in the Hand" after Convenant Woman and does not list out the final songs.
