One of my all-time favorite boots is Farewell Bloomfield. Although the sound is a little muddy, as 
reported at various review sites. It has the added bonus of containing Mike Bloomfields last performance 
on LARS and Grooms Still Waitin'.And it has City of Gold. Plus tons of alternate lyrics throughout.
But it was also incomplete, missing Senor, Covenant Woman, Love Minus Zero/No Limit and the gospel songs from
the show. On the official boot Ain't Gonna Go To Hell for Anybody is missing the first verse and more
but it's complete on the audience tape I receieved from Les Kokay this winter. This one is a prize.
