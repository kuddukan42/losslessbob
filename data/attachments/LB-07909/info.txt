Two soundboard tracks from Edinburgh 660520.
1- One Too Many Mornings
2- Like A Rolling Stone

These were broadcast about March 1969 on a pair of shows hosted by the late Scott Muni, a disk jockey at WNEW radio NYC.

Torrent contains two (!) different airchecks. 
The first (SG version) has a missing piece near the end of track-2 caused by a tape runout. The 2nd (PW version) is complete but has a duller sound and a bit more tape damage. (The first version has been bootlegged.)

To anyone who was friends with Mr. Muni, please contact me.
