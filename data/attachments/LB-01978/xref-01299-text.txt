Perhaps compiled by Mr. Les Kokay (unverified)


This torrent contains ALL the Bob Dylan tracks that have appeared on BobDylan.com. 

These tracks have all been obtained directly with XFileGet / Ra2Wav and more recently using Total Recorder. 
None of these tracks have been lifted from the Bootlegs "From the Vaults Vol 1 or 2" or "BobDylan.com Masters", 
on which the recordings have been re-edited to make the intros overlap.  Some tracks have occured more than once, 
and I have retained these 'errors' for the sake of completeness, except in the case of the large 60th Birthday repost, 
which I have ignored, as there was nothing new. Similiarly I have ignored the two videos and the Custom-Mix CDs. 
Many of these tracks are available elsewhere in better quality. The official released tracks are all available 
in 'lossless' format. I believe that all the audience recordings, shown as (A), have also circulated from the source 
tapes in 'lossless' format. 

Never-the-less this set documents ALL 105 tracks in best possible quality.
