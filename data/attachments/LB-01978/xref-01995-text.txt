Complete Bob Dylan.com (8 CDs)

This data DVD contains ALL the Bob Dylan tracks that have appeared on BobDylan.com.

These tracks have all been obtained directly with XFileGet / Ra2Wav and more recently using Total Recorder. None of these tracks have been lifted from the Bootlegs "From the Vaults Vol 1 or 2" or "BobDylan.com Masters" (on which the recordings have been re-edited to make the intros overlap. I have not included any tracks that don't feature Bob Dylan (the various covers are left out). Some tracks have occured more than once, and I have retained these 'errors' for the sake of completeness, (except in the case of the large 60th Birthday repost, which I have ignored, as there was nothing new).Similiarly I have ignored the two videos and the Custom-Mix CDs). Many of these tracks are available elsewhere in better quality (The official released tracks are all available in 'lossless' format). I believe that all the audience recordings (shown as (A)) have also circulated from the source tapes in 'lossless' format. 

Never-the-less this set documents ALL 96 tracks in best possible quality.

Original Notes
This torrent contains ALL the Bob Dylan tracks that have appeared on BobDylan.com.

These tracks have all been obtained directly with XFileGet / Ra2Wav and more recently using Total Recorder. None of these tracks have been lifted from the Bootlegs "From the Vaults Vol 1 or 2" or "BobDylan.com Masters" (on which the recordings have been re-edited to make the intros overlap. I have not included any tracks that don't feature Bob Dylan (the various covers are left out). Some tracks have occured more than once, and I have retained these 'errors' for the sake of completeness, (except in the case of the large 60th Birthday repost, which I have ignored, as there was nothing new).Similiarly I have ignored the two videos and the Custom-Mix CDs). Many of these tracks are available elsewhere in better quality (The official released tracks are all available in 'lossless' format). I believe that all the audience recordings (shown as (A)) have also circulated from the source tapes in 'lossless' format. 

Never-the-less this set documents ALL 96 tracks in best possible quality.
