This is the 20th Show of Bob Dylan's 1984 tour with former Rolling Stones guitarist 
Mick Taylor.  The band seems to have settled into a temporary state of complacently and 
routine.  Both this show and the show before (Milan) are very solid there just seems to be 
something missing; inspiration at the beginning of the shows.  Dylan builds his vocals 
and continues until the end of the show.  He repeats the line "I'm leaving my heart here 
with you" in the song "Simple Twist of Fate" during the verses.  Mick Taylor's playing 
seems to mirror Dylan's building during the show.  Listen to Taylor on "Every Grain of 
Sand" it's great.


Right now is the 20th Anniversary of those shows and I thought it would be cool to 
release each show to STG on the date of the actual show plus 20 years.  So, here is the 
twentieths show; only 7 more!!!  We'll see if I can keep up with the rest of the tour.  So 
far so good!
