I received this fairly low generation show in a tape trade about 20 years ago. 
I have not seen it posted here yet, so I recently converted my tape. 
This is a great recording, with all of the instruments and vocals very clear in the mix. 
It has a crisp and full sound with no audience interference, so I would give it at least an A-. 
Listen to the sample., The show is similar to the other '86 shows, although I am assuming that 
they play more extended jams than usual, because I could not fit this on two CDs, and most of 
the other shows I have seen from this tour do fit on two.
