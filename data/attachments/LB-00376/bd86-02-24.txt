Someone's Review:
This is a fantastic package from Japan. Although a third of these tracks 
have been made available in the past as a Westwood One radio broadcast, an 
HBO video, and on a variety of bootlegs, this is the first appearance of 
the complete concert. The stereo soundboard recording is amazing.
