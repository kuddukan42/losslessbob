Bob Dylan 
Dallas Music Complex
Dallas, Texas
7 November 1995
 
SOURCE: AUD recordings of unknown generation 
LINEAGE: CDR>EAC>WAV>MKW>SHN  
NOTES: excellent- sound; 
This is the complete Dallas show, compiled from two different sources.  The primary recording
is a high quality digital source which is unfortunately missing a few minutes of "It Ain't Me Babe" 
and "RDW."  These tracks are included in a seperate folder and come from an inferior analogue 
sourced recording.  I have not merged two sources together to create one complete (seamless) show 
but others may prefer to do that.
