       Well, I know this is a mouthful!!!  This torrent contains two separate sources for the same show. 
I recorded this night with both an ALD receiver and my mics.  There are parts that sound clearer listening
to the ALD source and some sound better on the Audience source.
       I guess a Matrix of both recordings would have been a better choice, but I think
both sources stand on their own.  You'll notice the times of the songs are a bit different
in length between the sources and track points were made as close to each other as possible.
    The Dead are allowing alternate sources to their Soundboards to be traded and Robert Hunter
is selling shows from 2004, not 2003, so this doesn't violate "Dime" rules. 
     Included in this torrent is complete artwork for both sources, a poster for the show, but I seem
 to have lost the ticket stub scan(although the stub is part of the artwork).
