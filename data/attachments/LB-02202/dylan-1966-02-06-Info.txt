Bob Dylan, Pittsburgh, PA, February 6, 1966 - Flac files
Audience recording

originally posted by pjl to alt.binaries.music.shn around August 2002
 These were posted as SHN files.  I decoded them to WAV and encocded them to FLAC 1.1.1 with some simple tags.
 I changed the filenames and created an md5 file and a Flac fingerprint file (FFP.txt).
