BOB DYLAN
Prague, Czech Republic
13th March 1995

Good quality first generation audience recording, transferred from cassette tape via Tascam DR07, WAV file edited 
in Audio Cleaning Lab. A DVD of this show already exists on this tracker, this is audio only from an unknown audio taper.

These early shows of the 1995 European tour were interesting for the fact that Bob was suffering from a back injury and/or 
some gastric ailment which meant that he could not play guitar for long. Thus plenty of harmonica, being the only 
instrument he had left to play in the pre-keyboard days. Also more concentration on vocal rendition which makes 
for some very interesting versions.
