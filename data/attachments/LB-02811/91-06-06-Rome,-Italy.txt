Concert # 302 of The Never-Ending Tour.  First concert of the 1991 Summer Tour Of Europe. 1991 concert # 39.

 

Concert # 18 with the 5th Never-Ending Tour band: Bob Dylan (vocal & guitar), John Jackson (guitar), Tony Garnier (bass), Ian Wallace (drums).
