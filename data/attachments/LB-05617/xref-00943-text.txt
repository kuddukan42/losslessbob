Bob Dylan - Rolling Thunder Revue 1975-10-31 "new" tape -- Plymouth, MA (FLAC)

A few months back, a small collection of line recordings from Dylan's fall 1975 Rolling Thunder tour surfaced (here), accompanied by a howling awful description that most Dylan collectors found so dubious that they ignored the fileset entirely. (Among other things, the description dated the recordings to 1976, described them all as previously uncirculating, and botched many titles. It also described the source as the unidentified "BGP" archive.)

http://www.thetradersden.org/forums/...ad.php?t=15402

It was torrented again (at BootCity) a few weeks back as "Rolling Thunder Redux," (and great thanks should go out to 15jugglers for uploading it!) and at that point several of us gave it a closer look, in part because "BGP" looked a lot like an acronym for "Bill Graham Productions," and that ties to Wolfgang's Vault, and Wolfgang's Vault did have some 1975 RTR recordings that seemed to be previously uncirculating. After some review, the collection, while not living up to its description, did include several previously uncirculating tracks from the line/SBD recording of the October 31, 1975 Plymouth RTR show.

http://www.bootcity.org/showthread.php?t=9270

So: Here we have a compilation of the "best quality" tracks from the "new" tape and the already circulating LK line recording of the show. All the Plymouth tracks from the new tape are included.

The remainder of the "new" tape came from the Waterbury RTR line recording, in no better quality than the already circulating edition of the show. (And not including either of the two missing Dylan tracks from that line recording.)

If you've already downloaded the DIME-safe version of the torrent, you need only tracks 0 and 16. If you have downloaded "Rolling Thunder Redux," you need only tracks 6, 7, 8, 9, 15, and 16 -- but you will need to rename the older .flac files so they'll sort/burn in the right order automatically.
