These two compilations contain all the different songs from each leg in approximate concert order. 
If a song was performed more than once, I have (subjectively) chosen the version that sounds best to my ears .
The songs that were played in the same slot night after night were left out, so if you have any 
one of these concerts you can add the 'filler' back in!

I've unfortunately lost the details of exactly which concert from which I sourced each track. Some will be
obvious (one offs), and some will be easy to identify from the recording characteristics, but 
others will require some careful listening to ID them properly.

Just treat these disks as a 'super concert' from each leg, it would be the sort of concert that
Dylan would do if the only played one concert per month. (if only...)
I think I included at least one track from every concert in the leg.

I find that when I listen to concert after concert, the 'dross' numbers tends to overshadow these little gems,
so these compilations are always a more enjoyable listening experience than listening to individual concerts.
