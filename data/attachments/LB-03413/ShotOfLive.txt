Review: 
This set is fairly good. The package aesthetics are nice. The photo images are blurred, but whether this is an artistic choice or simply bad images is uncertain. The silk screened silver discs are lackluster. To paraphrase one review, the sound quality of this audience recording  is very listenable. Not great ... but not at all bad.
