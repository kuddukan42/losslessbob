From the Olof's site: 
"Dylan choose to appear as the rejuvenated legend, rather punkish, in sleeveless shirts and leather vests, lot of amulets and one eardrop. 
The tour was a result of his collaboration with Tom Petty & The Heartbreakers at the Farm Aid benefit in September 1985. It was a tour that featured many of the new songs from EMPIRE BURLESQUE as well as songs from his entire career and then also a lot of rock 'n' roll covers."
