The Stadionsporthalle in Hannover was one of the ugliest places that I've ever been to see a concert. But the show was fine and (my first and only) 'Ring them Bells' made it sensational. 
Although levels are a bit low, the sound of this recording is very good when turning up the volume of your stereo. Seems that the guy at the board was stunned too, because the boost of 'Country Pie' comes 15 seconds late.
I unfortunately don't have the circulating LB-1155, a Schubert-recorded-ASM, and therefore can't compare, (but be grateful for an upload...), so I'll post some samples.

Enjoy!
