General comments on the series:
In the belief that Bit Torrent is an extremely effective means of sharing the music of
Bob Dylan, I will be seeding over the next months (while I have an optimal internet 
situation) a number of excellent recordings in most cases derived from cdrs/shn files 
off the masters. It has been my practice to identify such recordings by means of the use of
initials or some acronym etc. However, until I can make certain that the tapers involved
do not object to the use of such identificational strategies, I will use totally arbitrary 
letters to identify the tapers involved. 

There will be three series: (a) Legendary Tapers Series: tapers who may still be at work 
today, but whose recordings go back to the 1970’s and 1980’s; (b) NET Tapers Series: tapers
who may still be at work, but whose work essentially begins with the NET; (c) Contemporary 
Tapers Series: tapers whose work is rather recent. The Tapers will be identified as LTA, 
LTB…, NTA, NTB…, CTA, CTB… Nothing is implied by the letter of the alphabet used for a 
given taper. If permission to use a more memorable label is given, then I will append it 
to the above codes. 

Enjoy!
