Review: 
An excellent Japanese cd release of this 1980 Toronto soundboard recording. 
This show has been released on a few other titles over the years. 
It represents some of the best quality available from the early '80s gospel tour, 
and is an absolute must for anyone interested in the gospel material. 
This package is well done with the striking 'Rex Features' photo of Dylan in heavy mascara on the front, 
and a classy 'Retna Pictures' concert shot on the back. 
The unique title, and straight-forward typeset add to the overall aesthetic appeal.
