Show Information
This is a wonderful set of both of Dylan's 2000 performances at the Civic Auditorium in Santa Cruz, CA. Both shows are in excellent sound quality. There are some real gems on this one (check out the setlist). The CDs are split up into one for 03/15, one for 3/16, and one for the encores. Enjoy. Thanks to Bruno for the donation.

Extra Information
source: digital audience, recorded with schoeps mk-4 > DAT
digitally remastered by BRUNO on a MAC G4 - using BIAS PEAK & Steinberg Plugins
