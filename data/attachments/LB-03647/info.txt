Hi all, I'm happy to present to you a Bob Dylan performance from his 1966 tour to Melbourne, Australia. This concert was held at Festival Hall. It is the acoustic set he performed before The Band came on to perform electric.  

It could either be the April 19th or 20th show, I have done some research and found the following link:

http://www.bootlegarchive.com/media_description.asp?MediaID=727

Now, there is a release called Genuine Live 1966 (8 CD Bootleg), which has a show called "The Children's Crusade" on it that is apparentally the April 20th show. 

I do not have access to this boot, but just in case this show is from a different night, I am posting anyway. If anyone can advise whether this is a repeat of the bootleg above, let me know, otherwise enjoy the show. Sorry, do not have access to the 2nd set with The Band!

This comes from an Australian radio broadcast, A friend of mine was able to transfer the concert from Reel to WAV for me, and very kindly allowed me to post it here!
