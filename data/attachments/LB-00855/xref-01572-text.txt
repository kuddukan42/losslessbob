=== bd92-03-18 - Perth - MK.txt

I'm sharing some concerts from the 1992 Australian Tour that I have received from a friend.
He received them straight from Les Kokay, who has agreed to openly publish them.
The information provided by Les Kokay says:

"These MK (Michael Krogsgaard) shows are all originals. I got the cassettes from MK himself, and made them into files on my computer (probably using my Nakamichi B300 deck)
I used to use CoolEdit pro to split them up, but that produced BSE's and others removed the errors hence some LB numbers differ."

As for me, I've only renamed the files with their respective dates, respecting its original format without making any changes to them.
Thanks to the taper (MK), to LK for making them available, and to my friend Marcos for sharing them with me. chambre / May 2015

xxxxx

Perth, West Australia, Australia
18 March 1992

2.Lay Lady Lay

6.Dolly Dagger (Jimi Hendrix)

9.Mr. Tambourine Man

12.It Ain't Me, Babe
13.Cat's In The Well

16.Highway 61 Revisited

17.Ballad Of A Thin Man
18.Rainy Day Women # 12 & 35

Concert # 365 of The Never-Ending Tour. First concert of the 1992 Tour Of Australia. First 1992 concert.
First concert with the 6th Never-Ending Tour band:  Bob Dylan (vocal & guitar), Bucky Baxter (pedal steel guitar & electric slide guitar), John Jackson (guitar), Tony Garnier (bass), Ian Wallace (drums).
9, 10  Bob Dylan (vocal & acoustic guitar).
11, 12  acoustic with the band.

Note. Live debuts of Dolly Dagger, Little Maggie and Cats In The Well.
