Another excellent release from Rattlesnake, the new kid on the
block. The Minneapolis '98 show in absolutely astounding sound
quality from the board.....every bit the equal in sound to the Irving
Plaza show captured best on "White Dove" and "Tonight I'll Be
Playing Here for you."  Here's the one to have from Fall '98,
appended with five remastered filler tracks from the San Jose
May '98 soundboard. A dazzling package with stunning 12-page
color booklet and short notes on the show.  TrackList:
