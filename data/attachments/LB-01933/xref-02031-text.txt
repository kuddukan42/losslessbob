======

1-2 Bob solo acoustic
3 with Bobby Neuwirth
8-10 with Joan Baez
19 with Roger McGuinn
20 ensemble

======

audiowhore repair project - 1976

source used: LB-1933

Notes about this recording:
- all thanks to the taper for being there
- mono recording
- pitch was too low and speed too slow
- i found that the sound altered throughout from being between 0.2 Semitones to 0.3 Semitones off pitch
- Nothing from this show has been officially released.


What i have done:
- adjust pitch and speed for each song individually to correct
- fix SBEs
- eq mixing to better balance sound
- some hard limit on loud clapping
- tape flips, minor dropouts, microphone bumps cleaned/manually removed.
- No Noise or hiss reduction used,


Notes about versions of this show:
I think that there is only one source and that LB-1933, LB-2933, LB-3484 and LB-9153 are all derived from the same recording.
Please correct me if there is a second source.
