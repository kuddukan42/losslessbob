Discussion with reference to LB listings.
I suspect that the master source for this is the same as LB-698, which is (apparently incorrectly) identified as from the CB master. Like
LB-698 it contains the track "Just My Imagination" and lacks the track "Why Do I Have to Choose?" which appears on LB-1809 from
the Fendert STG series (where it is observed that sources do not list this song as being played on 6/9) and on the bootleg LB-2851, where
"Why Do I Have to Choose?" is recognized to be an addition from the following evening's show. The LB-698 mentions a cut at d2t11 4:41; 
the present first generation source does not have the same tracking as LB-698 and I did not observe any cut. (I do not have LB-698 on 
hand to compare, but there are many fingerprints that could establish whether the ultimate source of the two recordings is the same; nor
can I therefore compare the sound quality).
