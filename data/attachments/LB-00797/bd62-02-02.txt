Bob Dylan, 2/2/62, Harry Belafonte Sessions for Midnight Special, Webster Hall, NYC, 1CDR

(39min), excellent sound, dylan on harmonica, Dundas reports this as 12/61

many takes of Midnight Special (Takes 1-20)

Harry Belafonte (vocal), Ernie Calabria (guitar), Millard Thomas (guitar), Bob Dylan (harmonica), Norman Keenan (bass), Percy Bruce (drums), Don Lamond (drums), Danny Barrajanos (drums).
